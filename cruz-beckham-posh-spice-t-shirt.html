Cruz Beckham pays homage to mum Victoria Beckham in vintage 'Posh Spice' tee - here's where you can

 Posted on September 10, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  430 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The Beckham clan are noteworthy when it comes to publicly supporting one another’s personal ventures. But we have reason to believe that Cruz Beckham – alongside showing support for his mother –  is a Spice Girls superfan.

The Beckhams' youngest son posted an Instagram story on Monday wearing a white 'Posh Spice' tee, which contains a throwback image of Victoria wearing her iconic slinky black slip dress and dark bobbed hair.

Cruz's t-shirt sports a vintage 'Posh Spice' image 

 Cruz paired the vintage t-shirt with gold jewellery and a double denim ensemble – a style he also wore to Victoria Beckham's after party for her Paris Fashion Week debut earlier this year. Posh Spice reposted the short video to her own story, accompanied with a cute smiling Bitmoji throwing up a peace sign.

This isn’t the first time the 17 year-old has uploaded a photo of himself supporting the iconic 90's band. In August, he posted an Instagram story wearing another white 'Spice Girls' t-shirt, with images of the five band members and their respective signatures, nodding to their debut album cover Spice – a shirt that has appeared on his Instagram feed twice since August.

A trend-setter in his own right, he has made a mark with his individual street style aesthetic, unafraid to experiment with colours and chunky accessories. Not yet 18, he has already graced the cover of i.D, mirroring a shot of his father David Beckham from the cover of Homme Plus in 2000, both of which were photographed by Steven Klein.
