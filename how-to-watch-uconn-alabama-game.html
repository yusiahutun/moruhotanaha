While March Madness is coming to a close, there’s still more action to see. The Final Four tournament of the college basketball extravaganza will see the Connecticut Huskies and Alabama Crimson Tide face off to decide who makes it to the National Championship. If you’re wondering how to watch the UConn vs. Alabama game live for free, we’ve got you covered.

The NCAA Division I men’s basketball tournament, commonly referred to as March Madness, is a single-elimination tournament that crowns the men’s college basketball national champion for the Division I level in the National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA). The tournament started in 1939, is played in the month of March, and features 68 teams. The 85th annual March Madness tournament kicked off on March 19 and will end in a championship game on April 8 at State Farm Stadium in Glendale, Arizona.

Last weekend’s Elite Eight determined which teams would move on to the Final Four; UConn overcame Illinois 77-52, while Alabama came out on top against Clemson 89-82. Heading into March Madness, UConn was No. 1 on the seeds list with a 31-3 record and Alabama was No. 16 with a 21-11 record. The other two teams competing in the Final Four on April 6 are Purdue and NC State.

If you’re repping the Huskies or Crimson Tide in the Final Four of March Madness, keep scrolling to learn how to watch the UConn vs. Alabama game live for free to see who’s headed to the National Championship.

When is the UConn vs. Alabama game?

The UConn vs. Alabama game is on Saturday, April 6 at 8:49 p.m. ET. March Madness started on March 19 and ends on April 8. The UConn vs. Alabama game takes place right after Purdue and NC State battle it out at 6:09 p.m. ET that same day. The winners of both games will earn spots in the National Championship.

  • First Four: March 19-20
  • First round: March 21-22
  • Second round: March 23-24 
  • Sweet 16: March 28-29 
  • Elite Eight: March 30-31 
  • Final Four: Saturday, April 6
  • NCAA Championship: Monday, April 8
