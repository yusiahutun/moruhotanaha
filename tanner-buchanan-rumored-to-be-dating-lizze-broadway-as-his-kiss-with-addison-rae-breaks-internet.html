Actors Tanner Buchanan and Addison Rae left people shocked as they shared a kiss onstage during the 2021 MTV Movie & TV Awards on May 16. The award show took place at the Hollywood Palladium in Los Angeles and fans were pretty excited about it as they witnessed a starry event after a long period of time due to the Covid-19 pandemic. But a kiss between Buchanan and Rae, who are co-starring on ‘He’s All That’, was not something people were expecting.

According to reports, both actors were present at the stage to give the Best Kiss award, but before reading out the name of the winners — Madelyn Cline and Chase Stokes — the pair went on to passionately kiss each other, sending the Internet into a frenzy. It also sparked a lot of questions, like whether the two are currently dating anyone else or if they have a secret romance brewing between them. MTV also posted a tweet sharing the clip of the highlight of the night. It tweeted, “Confirmed: the chemistry between @HesAllThatMovie castmates @whoisaddison & @_TannerBuchanan is!!!!”

Who is Tanner Buchanan’s girlfriend?

Buchanan gained popularity as Robby Keene in ‘Cobra Kai’ and as rumors are to be believed, he is in a relationship with Lizze Broadway. They were seen together in the 2019 San Diego Comic-Con, where they shared a behind-the-scenes vlog of the time they spent with the cast during the Netflix show’s panel. Besides, Buchanan and Broadway have a YouTube channel together named ‘Lizze & Tanner’ which signals that they are dating. Though Tanner mostly posts photos of his set on his social media page, he once shared a picture of candles and rose petals on the floor and wrote in the caption, “What you do when the person you love comes to visit. @lizzebroadway.”

Who is Lizze Broadway?

Broadway is a 23-year-old actress, who has worked in ‘The Rookie’, ‘American Pie Presents: Girls' Rules’, ‘Splitting Up Together’, and ‘Here and Now’. The Ohio-born recently got her big break as a lead in the ‘The Boys’ spinoff to play a superhero character named Emma.
