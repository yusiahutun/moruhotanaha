Duck Dynasty cast members officially left reality TV after growing into household names. Thanks to the Robertson family's hilarious antics, the show has remained memorable in the minds of their fans. The reality television series gave the audience an inside look at the day-to-day activities of the family. It constitutes 11 seasons.

The show aired on A&E between 2012 and 2017, portraying the life of Louisiana natives who attained financial success thanks to their family business called Duck Commander. The company focuses on duck hunters' products, particularly the duck variety known as the Duck Commander.

The men in the Robertson family are brothers Si and Phil, alongside Phil's sons Jep Willie and Jase. All of them are notable for their long beards. The family is also known for its Evangelical Christian views.

Willie Robertson

Most people know Willie as the star of the TV series Duck Dynasty. However, he is also an avid outdoorsman, dad to five, author (New York Times bestseller) the CEO of Buck Commander and Duck Commander.

Phil established Duck Commander in 1973, but Willie Robinson later joined the company as president. He transformed the enterprise from a simple river-side business to an excellent destination for everything outdoors.

He worked with his wife Korie to author The Duck Commander Family: How Faith, Family, and Ducks Built a Dynasty. The book has stayed on the list of New York Times bestsellers for a record over 35 weeks. Willie is a father of five children: Sadie, John Luke, Bella, Will, and Rebecca.

Silas Merritt 'Si' Robertson

For a long time, fans have considered Uncle Si to be the breakout star of Duck Dynasty. He has also made appearances in several other shows like Buck Commander, and Last Man Standing. The star's personality has earned him "Going Si-Ral", a short-lived spinoff where he gave commentary on viral videos.

Si and Phil are brothers, best friends, and partners in crime. His specific role in the workshop is to fashion reeds to be inserted in all duck calls. The Vietnam veteran often shares his war stories, and this sometimes gets the crew behind schedule.

Si is also an author. He published his 2013 autobiography called Si-Cology 1, a New York Times bestseller. He is married to Christine, and the two have a pair of grown children: Scott and Trasa, each with four sons. In total, Si has eight grandsons.

Jase Robertson

Jase is Kay Robertson and Phil Robertson, aka The Duck Commander's son. He appears as himself in Duck Dynasty, and his role in the family-run enterprise involves the production of Duck Calls. He works in the call room with his brother Jep and Uncle Si, alongside Godwin and Martin who are employees of the business.

Jase was born in Louisiana's West Monroe. He was raised there alongside his brothers Willie, Jep, and Allan Robertson. The Duck Dynasty star is known to come up with different tactics for wasting time at work with some colleagues. Willie, his brother, and CEO of the family business is always annoyed by this.

Despite the small conflict, Jase is always out for the best. He prefers calling the swamp his office and is in charge of the business' manufacturing segment. His job is to invent, fine-tune, and fabricate new duck calls. He is married to his high school sweetheart, Missy. The couple has been together for over two decades, and have three kids: Mia, Cole, and Reed.

Jep Robertson

Jules Jeptha "Jep" Robertson is the youngest child in Kay's and Phil's household. He was raised in the duck blind and is currently the editor and cameraman for Duck Commander. He has years of industry experience and uses this in capturing the best footage for the Duckmen DVD series.

Jep Robertson lives with his spouse Jessica. The couple has five children called Jules Augustus, River, Priscilla, Merritt, and Lily.

Phil Robertson

Phil was born and raised in the Vivian community of Louisiana and has always had hunting in his blood since he was young. After spending several years as a teacher in several schools within the state, he and his family embarked on a mission to convert the passion for fishing and hunting into an income source.

He got the support of Kay, his wife, and four sons, Jep, Willie, Jase, and Alan. He didn't find the duck calls that existed in the market to be satisfactory, so he started practising making calls that produce an accurate duck sound. Ultimately, he resigned from his coaching career to enjoy his duck hunting passion.

That is how Duck Commander came into being in 1973. Since then, the products have sold in all U.S. states and other countries across the border.

Kay Robertson

She is the family's revered matriarch, and whatever she declares goes. Kay is Phil's wife, mother to Jase, Jep, Willie, and Alan. She is also the mother in law of Missy, Lisa, Jessica, and Korie. She mostly keeps her husband and the boys from staying in the woods for too long, always welcoming them back with a home-cooked meal every night.

She doesn't keep her cooking prowess to herself since she believes that talent is a gift to be shared with others. It is not rare to find her feeding the entire family and other people within the neighbourhood. Kay's famous dishes are fried deer steak, banana pudding, sticky frog legs, and crawfish pie.

Korie Robertson

She isn't originally from a family of hunters, but she fits perfectly with the Robertsons. Korie is married to Willie, who is also her business partner. She played a crucial role to ensure the business grows into a continually expanding company. Korie is the office manager at Duck Commander, always there to ensure the workstation does not become a funhouse.

Korie met Willie when she was still in third grade, and got married just a year after leaving high school. They are parents to five children: Rowdy, Bella, Lil'Will, Sadie, Rebecca, and John Luke.

Jessica Robertson

Jessica was raised as a hunter, and she grew up spending the weekends in the woods hunting alongside her father. She also has a vast business background, and that is why she fits right in with the family. She is Jep's wife, and the two met in 2001.

Previously, Jessica worked in the real estate sector. She is a licensed agent, with vast experience in sales. She currently works at Duck commander and has five children with her husband. These are Jules Augustus, River, Merritt, Lily, and Priscilla.

Missy Robertson

Missy is married to Jase Robertson and is the mother of Mia, and her brothers, Cole and Reed. The two have been together for more than two decades. Missy loves music and spends most of her time songwriting and singing. She also volunteers as a music teacher at the school where her children learn, as well as the crafts director at Camp Ch-Yo-Ca for the last five summers.
