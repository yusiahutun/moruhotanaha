The first date can be nerve-cracking, and it is normal to feel anxious. There is so much to think about, including what you will wear. You do not want to screw up your outfit and make the wrong first impression. If you have scored a first date with a man and have no idea what to wear, the first date outfits that guys love should be an inspiration.

When it comes to the first date, dressing for the occasion matters. Therefore, you need to choose your outfit based on the location. For instance, you should not dress up in a skirt suit when the venue of the first date is a bar. Unfortunately, the biggest mistake most women make is to throw something on at the last minute before heading to the first date location.

2. Matching top and skirt

This is one of the most trendy outfits for the first date. A simple skirt that fits your body nicely with a matching camisole can create a chic look. 3. Midi dress with sneakers

A midi dress reads feminine but not overly sweet when paired with white sneakers for a relaxed first date. The midi dresses exude easy romance and are the ideal first date outfits for midsummer. You can also dress up in a midi-dress if you are going to a romantic evening dinner. If you expect the first date to extend until evening, you can add a leather jacket to the look.

4. Jumpsuit

If you are in a rush to go for a dinner date, you can dress up in a jumpsuit. An elegant jumpsuit that fits your body nicely will make you look relaxed yet elegant for the first date. You can spice up the look with metallic stilettos and a pair of loops. You should stick to minimal yet cute date outfit ideas for this outfit, especially for the first date.

5. Corset top and dress

The corset-style dress is an ideal option if you have scored a first date with your crush. You can choose the corset dress for casual day outings or a beautiful date night in springtime. There is a range of options for this look, from go-to tops to beautiful satin dresses.

6. Blazer, casual tee, and jeans

The blazer and jeans combo is an ideal outfit if you are going to head straight to the first date venue after a long day at work. You should dress up in denim jeans and casual shoes if the occasion is going to be low-key. If you want to inject some personality into this look, you can add accessories such as a statement bag or chic look. A bright coloured blazer will lift the look.

7. Skater dress with sneakers

This is one of the simplest outfits for the first date that will ensure you get many compliments. You should wear a fun, bright midi dress with a subtle print. Then, pair the skater dress with your favourite sneakers, and you are good to go. This laid-back and chic look will leave a good first impression.

11. Pleated skirt with top

If you want to add a European touch to your first date outfit, you should dress up in a pleated skirt with a matching top. 12. Shirt dress with belt

Every girl has a shirt dress with a belt in her wardrobe. A shirt dress with a belt always works wonders on the first date. For this occasion, you can spice up the look with a pair of loop and heel or knee-length boots. You should also carry a nice bag to finish up the look. 13. Sweater dress

Can you dress up in a sweater dress for your first date? The answer to this question is a big yes. You cannot dress up in a camisole top when going for a date night in the cold winter season. A warm sweater dress is an ideal choice. You can pair it with knee-length boots or a wide strap belt to make it more stylish.

14. Full sleeve high neck black top with jeans

A black high-neck top with dark blue jeans will speak volumes about your personality. This look will leave a good first impression, especially on a winter date night. It will create the impression that you are trying too hard or trying to be too easy. You can wear it with white sneakers and a classic handbag.

15. Denim shorts with a silk camisole

The cute denim shorts offer a contemporary vibe for a beach date or when it is too hot to wear jeans. Pairing denim shorts with a silk camisole will create a look that's both relaxed and polished. You can finish it all off with some gorgeous sandals and a straw hat.

Frequently asked questions

  • Are leggings OK on a first date? There is nothing wrong with wearing leggings on your first date. You can wear them as long as you match them with the right top and pair of heels.
  • What should your wear to meet a guy for the first time? If you are going to meet up with a guy for the first time, you can dress up in a little black dress or a casual blouse and jeans finished with feminine accessories.
  • Are jeans acceptable on a first date? Denim is approved for the first date scenario as it matches many outfits. Avoid jeans that have too many wear marks or holes in them.
  • Should I tuck my shirt in on a first date? Unless you're wearing a tie, you should leave the shirt untucked. Tucking in your shirt will add that little bit of formality that you don't need.
  • What should you not wear on a first date? You should never dress in a suit and tie on a first date. This gives your crush the impression that you have just come from work.
  • Can I wear a suit on a first date? Wearing a suit on a first date is not an excellent option as it will make you feel stuffy. Instead, you should go for a more casual outfit.
  • What jeans should I wear on a first date? If you choose to wear jeans on your first date, you should go for light-wash weekend denim. It is universally flattering and will work with a variety of tops.
