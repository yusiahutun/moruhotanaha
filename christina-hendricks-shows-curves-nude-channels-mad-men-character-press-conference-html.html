Her character Joan Harris is undoubtedly the sexiest character on Mad Men, and perhaps even on TV at the moment.

And it seems actress Christina Hendricks, who plays the screen siren, has been taking style tips from the office manager.

The 37-year-old stole the show when the female cast members got together for a press conference on Monday, dressed in a flattering nude coat dress that accentuated her famous curves.

Stunning: Christina Hendricks wowed in nude dress coat at a Mad Men press conference on Monday

Cinched in at the waist with a belt, the sixties style dress looked fabulous against her pale skin.

And ensuring she looked every inch a bombshell she teamed the nude number, which could look dull on someone else, with a pair of open-toe leopard print heels and dash of bright red lipstick.

Her striking lips looked incredible against the contrast of her famous red hair, while her eyes were accentuated by a slick of black eyeliner.

Red head: Christina's red hair which was styled in waves looked striking against her red lipstick

Just like her character Christina made the other women present almost fade into the background in comparison.

Pretty January Jones couldn’t have looked further away from her character Betty Francis, dressed in a pair of patterned white jeans, with a white T-shirt and a red leather jacket.

Her cropped blonde hair looked a little messy and she wore a simple pair of black flat boots.

Look-a-like: It looks like Christina has been taking style tips from her character Joan (R)

Elisabeth Moss, who plays one of the most integral characters on the show, Peggy Olson, opted for a simple black dress with racer style straps.

She completed her chic look with a pair of heels and a slick of deep pink lipstick, while her hair was pulled off her face. 

Season six of Mad Men will premiere on AMC in the U.S. on April 7 and the cast have already been spotted filming. 

Co-stars: Elisabeth Moss (L) and January Jones (R) who also star on the show opted for very different looks

Speaking  about the show creator Matthew Weiner also confirmed that this would be the penultimate season - with season seven the last.

'I’m going to confirm that,' said Weiner.

Fortunately Christina has got plenty up her sleeve for when the hugely popular show comes to an end.

Filming: John Hamm, who plays Don Draper on the show was spotted filming for the new series which starts again in April

After taking on a number of supporting roles in various films, she is now set to be a leading lady. 

As well as being the star of Ryan Gosling’s directorial debut How to Catch a Monster, she will also be starring in A Book of Common Prayer. 

She will play Charlotte Douglas in the adaption of Joan Didion’s 1977 novel of the same name, which will be directed by Campbell Scott. 

Her character’s life is torn apart when her daughter runs away with a group of terrorist radicals, forcing her to go and try to find her in Central America, where revolution is never far away.

Scott and producer Marcia Weiss said in a statement: ‘We are thrilled that Christina has agreed to come on board to play the lead role of Charlotte Douglas.

‘Charlotte is a complex and compelling character and Christina is the perfect actor to play her.’
