See Zendaya's Vera Wang Dress at Challengers Los Angeles Premiere

 Posted on June 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  263 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Fans of Zendaya seemingly couldn't get enough of the actress' tennis whites and neon greens throughout the Challengers press tour, she decided to switch it up and put the sporty colors on the back burner for her look at the Los Angeles premiere of the movie.

Zendaya attended the carpet for the event, which took place at the Westwood Village Theater on April 16, in a custom black and pink Vera Wang gown. The dress' bodice featured intricate sheer lace detailing in black, and was cradled by black satin corset details at the waist before extending into a large blush pink skirt with a pop of black tulle.

The 27-year-old kept her beauty and accessories for the look rather minimal, keeping her hair in a loose updo, opting for a fresh face and wearing Bulgari jewelry.

"I've really enjoyed this press tour. I think just the idea of tennis whites in general and kind of playing with that in each of my looks," Zendaya told Entertainment Tonight about funneling in tennis themes into her Challengers press tour looks. "It's just been fun, you know, just experimenting and building this other red carpet character."

As for the Vera Wang gown specifically, the actress shared the piece had been sitting in her closet for quite some time. "It's interesting 'cause we've had this dress for a while and it just felt like the right time to bring her out," Zendaya said, hinting at a sexier scene in the movie that features a nightgown.

Challengers arrives in theaters on April 26.
