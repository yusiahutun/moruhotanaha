Daniel Moder is a cinematographer who has worked in the TV series, Dead to Me (2019) and The Last Tycoon (2016-2017). His cinematography credits also include movies like Ibiza (2018), Plush (2013), and The Hit (2007). 

For his work on the TV movie, The Normal Heart, he was also nominated for a Primetime Emmy in Outstanding Cinematography for a Miniseries or Movie category in 2014. 

Daniel Moder's Net Worth 

Daniel has a net worth of $10 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. 

Career Info

Daniel started his career in Hollywood as a production assistant; he worked in the movie, Crimson Tide, in 1995. A year later, he worked as a camera loader in the film, The Fan.

Daniel Moder's Wiki / Bio: Age

Daniel was born on 31st January 1969 in Los Angeles, California. His birth name is Daniel Richard Moder.

Daniel's father, Mike Moder, is a producer. His work includes TV series, namely The Wonderful World of Disney (1997) and Matlock (1986-1993). Mike has four more children. They are John, Jyl, Jane, and Debbie Moder.

Education

Daniel graduated from Saint Monica High School in 1987. He has a Psychology degree from the University of Colorado; he graduated in 1992.
