Shocked The Fans By The News Of Her Wedding

WWE superstar, Zelina Vega, lives a blissful married life with her spouse Aleister Black, a Dutch professional wrestler. Vega and Aleister married secretly in a private ceremony.

Wrestler Zelina Vega and her spouse Aleister Black SOURCE: Instagram (@zelina_vegawwe)

It took quite long for both of them to reveal the news to the public. The WWE superstar couple married in 2018, but they kept the details of their wedding under the radar.

Vega and Black were first spotted together on the screen at the NTX Championship angle between Andrade Cien Almas and Black. In an episode of NTX, Zelina slapped Black, which created speculation about them having an affair. 

Zelina Vega and Aleister Black training together SOURCE: Instagram (@zelina_vegawwe)

But they gave a shock to their fans by tying the knot. The news of their news swirled soon throughout the media and became one of the most discussed topics. However, neither Vega nor Aleister officially confirmed the news.
