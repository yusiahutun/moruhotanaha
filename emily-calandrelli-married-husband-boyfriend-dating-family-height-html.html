Career and Progression:

Emily Calandrelli attended West Virginia University and became one of the Truman Scholars which enabled her to work in Washington, D.C. for the US Representative, Alan B. Mollohan. In 2013, she graduated from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology with a Master’s degree in Astronautics and also in Technology and Policy. She also worked as a Harvard NASA Tournament Lab visiting scholar.

Moreover, the TV host speaks and presents views on the topics of space exploration, scientific literacy and equality of sexes in STEM. Along with her job as a TV show presenter, she is also a contributing writer at ‘TechCrunch.' The reality show presenter started hosting Xploration Outer Space in the Fox Xploration Station education block since 2014. In April 2017, the show presenter has made her cameo appearance in the Netflix’s show ‘Bill Nye Saves the World’.
