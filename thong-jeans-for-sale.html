These $168 Thong Jeans Have A Wait-List

 Posted on August 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  306 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Last season, we reported The Great Jeans Apocalypse was among us when new designer Thibaut debuted no pants-pants (aka thong jeans) at Tokyo Fashion Week. Understandably, the internet quickly lost its cool over the extremely ripped and deconstructed jeans.

Splash News

Thibaut debuts thong jeans at Tokyo Fashion Week in October 2017.

For those who thought the jeans (or lack thereof) were just a runway gimmick, think again: LA-based brand Carmar Denim created their own thong jeans knockoff—and they're already sold-out. Yes, I repeat: a pair of $168 denim seams now has a wait-list of people who want to buy them.

Carmar’s Sold-Out "Extreme Cut Out Jean"
