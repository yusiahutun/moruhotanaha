Early Life

Jim Nantz was born on May 17, 1959, in Charlotte, North Carolina, United States of America. This makes him 63 years old as of 2022.

Education

Talking about his educational status, he did his schooling at Marbolo High School. Here, he was the co-captain of the basketball team.

After schooling, he attended the University of Houston in Texas and majored in broadcasting. While there, he was honored with a Doctor of Humane Letters.
