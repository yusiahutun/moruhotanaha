What is Jewish Matchmaking?

Jewish Matchmaking is a reality TV dating show hailing from the producers of Indian Matchmaking. The TV series revolves around Jewish singles of different races, ages, genders, and religious backgrounds from all over the world getting matched up by Aleeza Ben Shalom, a professional Jewish matchmaker and dating coach.

To find the right match, get married, and stay married, matchmaking clients depend on Shidduchim, a centuries-old matchmaking practice rooted in Orthodox Jewish tradition.

Before going on dates, the potential meets with the matchmaker to discuss what they are looking for. Aleeza then organizes fun dates for potential matches, such as:

  • Jet-skiing
  • Horseback riding
  • Roller-skating
