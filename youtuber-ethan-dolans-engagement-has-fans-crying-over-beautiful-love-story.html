YouTuber Ethan Dolan's engagement has fans 'crying' over 'beautiful' love story

 Posted on June 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  390 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Social media star Ethan Dolan, one half of the Dolan Twins, is engaged to his girlfriend Kristina Alice after four years of dating. Kristina shared the happy news on Instagram with a first look at her engagement ring.

Ethan, who rose to fame with his brother Grayson on Vine and YouTube, is engaged. The YouTuber and his girlfriend have kept their romance out of the social media spotlight and the engagement news left many of their longtime fans in happy tears.

Ethan Dolan and Kristina Alice are engaged

Congratulations are in order to Ethan and Kristina as the couple embarks on their next chapter in life.

Kristina announced their engagement on Instagram with an adorable picture next to beautiful scenery alongside the beautiful caption: “The most special day of my life.”

The couple’s faces are not visible in the gorgeous sunset image and they embraced in a tight hug featuring Kristina’s stunning engagement ring.

Ethan is yet to share the exciting news on his own social media profiles and many fans have requested more snaps from the “special day” and even a full proposal video.

Fans in happy tears over pair’s beautiful love

Kristina and Ethan’s fans are over the moon over their exciting news and many congratulated them on the important decision in their lives.

“They’re growing up, I can’t take it,” one fan wrote. “Still crying,” someone else said. “So incredibly happy for you two.”

“This is so special,” another one said. “Screaming, crying, jumping up and down so happy for you guys,” another user said.

“I am officially melting,” YouTube’s official Instagram page wrote. “Congratulations to you and Ethan!”

“The most beautiful couple in the world!! Thank you for taking care of [Ethan] when we couldn’t Kristina, we owe you the world,” another fan wrote.

Who is Kristina Alice?

Kristina, 25, is an online influencer from Australia with a fan base of 200,000 followers on Instagram. She shares snippets from her everyday life, travels, and fashion outfits.

Kristina and Ethan first sparked rumors after collaborating on the music video Hawaiian Party where they played a romantic couple.

They reportedly started dating in 2019. On October 13, 2020, the pair celebrated their first-year anniversary with a string of cute pictures. Kristina included a lengthy caption with the key lessons from their first year together.
