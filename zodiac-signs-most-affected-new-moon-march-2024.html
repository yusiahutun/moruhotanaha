4 Zodiac Signs Most Affected

The 28-day lunar cycle always begins with a new moon. Think of it as the first line of a story or the first few notes of a song. And if you’re one of the zodiac signs most affected by the new moon in Pisces, you’re on the precipice of so much change. This new moon will help you process the heavy energy that has been weighing on you, and although it may feel slow and extra dreamy, it represents the calm before the storm of eclipse season.

The two most climactic moments of the lunar cycle take place at the new moon and the full moon, acting as markers in time that highlight turning points in our lives. The full moon may get most of the applause, as its bright light always heightens the energy. The new moon is usually a much quieter experience, but that doesn’t take away from its importance. You may feel tired, hollow, and hopeful all at the same time, as the new moon forces you to make peace with the past and embrace a new whole chapter of your life. Something that begins under a new moon will always take shape by the time the corresponding full moon takes place six months later. The intentions you set during a new moon will help you break away from what is no longer in alignment with your path as you take the first steps toward everything that is.

While you don’t have control over the future, you are capable of influencing the outcome. And because this is the final new moon before eclipse season begins, this is your last opportunity to manifest before the Universe takes over the wheel and redirects your destiny.

Taking place on March 10 at 5:00 a.m. ET, the upcoming new moon takes place in wise, spiritual, empathetic, creative, imaginative, dreamy, sleepy, and forgiving Pisces, encouraging you to set aside your ego and surrender to a Universe that drags you back and forth like ocean currents. Because Pisces is traditionally ruled by Jupiter—planet of abundance and growth—this new moon will encourage more of whatever is on your mind and heart. Pisces is known for being porous and sensitive to external energies, so be cognizant of what you expose yourself to. Jupiter is currently moving through sensual Taurus, encouraging you to overcome anxiety by grounding yourself with a measured approach to material pleasure.

However, this new moon may leave you feeling groggy, confused, and out-of-sync with time and space. You might feel as though you’ve woken up from a nap that lasted much longer than intended, as though you can’t fully wake up from it. That’s because this new moon will be separating from a conjunction with heavy-handed Saturn and an applying conjunction to nebulous Neptune. During this new moon, you’ll be processing some intense psychic downloads, but the process may be more taxing to your spirit than expected. Even if nothing makes sense, know that it will soon.
