40 Thanksgiving Nail Designs to Try

 Posted on July 22, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1174 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The holidays are always a fun time to dress up your nails. You can adorn your manicure with tiny ornaments for Christmas or go sparkle-crazy for New Year's Eve. Thanksgiving nail designs, however, are a bit less obvious. The option is there to get literal with your designs (think: pumpkins and turkeys) or go for a less on-the-nose design that still captures the essence of the holiday.

"The best way to emulate Thanksgiving is to find inspiration from the season, the activities, or even the food you consume during this holiday," says celebrity nail artist San Sung Kim. "You can simply use earthy and warm tones of the season and add some Thanksgiving magic with simple designs such as pumpkins or leaves."

If you don't want something so literal, expert Amy Oung says you can stick to warm colors. "You can get into a festive mood by wearing rich neutral colors on the nails, says Oung. "Once you have the colors, you can matte, marbelize, add glitter, do abstract lines —the ideas are limitless." See for yourself, below.

Meet the expert

  • San Sung Kim is a celebrity nail artist who specializes in avant-garde, hand-painted designs.
  • Amy Oung is a nail artist and educator for Orly.

Fallen Leaves

“The chocolate brown tips and leafy cuffs are a subtle way to celebrate the season of Thanksgiving,” says Kim.

Shimmer Set

This warm, brownish-red shade — Stiletto on the Run by Orly — is perfect for this time of year. And the touches of gold foil and glitter up the festive factor.

Autumn Swirls

“For this Thanksgiving look, I added in fun details such as autumn swirls and forest details to capture the playful and warm mood of the season,” says Kim.

Autumnal Nudes

Lights Lacquer polishes were used to create this neutral look that leaves the half-moon at the base of the nail visible. In addition to looking gorgeous, the negative space also allows the nail to grow out beautifully.

Mauvey Browns

If you lean toward pink hues, this look is perfect for you. It features a blend of chocolate brown and mauve polishes from Luxio Plush ($18) to create a sweet, yet moody look.

Bordeaux Red

It doesn't get more quintessentially spooky season than a deep Bordeaux red. This shade, Luxio Vamp ($18) from Polished Pinkies, is worthy of heavy rotation.

Pumpkin Aura

These aura nails use orange at the center and fade out to yellow, creating a gorgeous pumpkin-y glow. The look was painted on the Aprés Sculpted Almond Medium ($17).

Dark Chocolate

These stunning nails were created using the Aprés Sculpted Square Long ($17) gel-x tips and the French Manicure Gel shade Osiris ($15). It features a glossy tip over a matte base.

Pop Art French

Cozy autumnal hues were used to create these playful pop art-inspired French tips. They're perfect if you want something that fits the season without being too Thanksgiving-y.

Cranberry Brie

These nails evoke the cranberry-baked brie that I can't wait to eat this Thanksgiving. It was painted with the Aprés French Manicure Gel Vege Mighty ($15) polish on top of the Sculpted Stiletto Medium ($17) gel-x tips.

Milk Chocolate

Carbon Coffee ($14) from Gel Baby LA is the perfect shade of brown to emulate milk chocolate. This look features a blank triangle at the base of the tip, which echoes the shape of the pointy almond tip.

Fall Tangerine

Simple lines in yellow, orange, red, and dark brown were used to create this striking graphic look.

Fall Florals

A buttery yellow, cranberry red, and pumpkin orange come together to make this fall floral design. Having the flowers on just one nail keeps the look feeling delicate.

Autumn Skittles

Although teal isn't usually viewed as a fall shade, it's the perfect bright addition to this fall manicure. It features the shades Fern ($19) and Jungle ($19) from The Gel Bottle Inc and Berries ($12), Chestnut ($12), and Mustard ($12) from Premier Gel.

Pumpkin Spice

If pumpkins are your jam, go literal with your Thanksgiving nail art. This look features pumpkin accent nails aside orange and beige solid nails.

Thanksgiving Gnome

Gnomes may be big for Christmas designs, but they've been slowly popping into other holidays, like Thanksgiving. This look features one gnome per hand, along with pumpkins and a solid green shade.

Bronze

The shade Lyra from Cirque Colors was used to create this stunning bronze look. It's the perfect polish to grab when you want an interesting look that requires minimal effort.

Checkered for Autumn

This checkered pattern uses light shades of orange, mauve, and brown to create this super pretty geometric look. Each nail is also sprinkled with a little bit of gold leaf to add some oomph.

Shimmer Harvest

Shimmering shades of autumnal red, green, orange, purple, and brown were used to create this vibrant look.

Pumpkin Patch

Go pumpkin crazy with this look featuring kitschy fall etchings and glittery shades of orange, green, and gold.

Candy Brown Shades

This neutral French tip design features three polish shades from gel Gel Care Official, including Girl Scout ($20), Archive Beige ($20), and Terracotta ($20). It's pretty any time of year, but works especially well for a minimalist Thanksgiving design.

Clean Lines

Clean lines are used to create this geometric look. While some nails feature a checkered pattern, others feature color in monochrome blocks.

Pumpkin French

Get the pumpkin vibes without going too literal. This design features a shimmery pumpkin orange micro tip and a simple dot at the base of each nail.

Matte Orange

An orange nail is a super simple way to approach your Thanksgiving manicure. The addition of the matte top coat adds an unexpected finish.

Fall Sunflower

Sunflowers scream autumn, so sporting a few on your nails is perfect for Thanksgiving. This look also features orange and gold glitter polishes.

Dark Chocolate

The polish Kafkaesque ($13) from Mooncat was used to create this deep dark brown nail. It's simply gorgeous.

Simply Fall

Polka dots are a year-round favorite. Consider incorporating them into an autumnal manicure like this one featuring brown, orange, and cream hues.

Pomegranate Plaid

Plaid is a fun way to enjoy design in a more minimalist spirit. This look is centered on a gorgeous pomegranate shade that's perfect for Thanksgiving.

Brown Ombré Tips

Instead of going for a traditional French tip with a clean arch, this look features a gradient of brown to sheer.

Neutral Abstract Florals

Polishes from Magpie Beauty were used to create this abstract neutral look that features abstract splotches of brown polish and tiny white daisies.

Glitter Pumpkin

Jazz up a pumpkin-orange look by reaching for this glittery number from Mooncat. It's the shade Magic Mushroom ($14).

Autumn Abstract

Tiny overlapping dots were used to create this abstract look. It uses a mix of red, green, yellow, black, and white polishes from Magpie Beauty.
