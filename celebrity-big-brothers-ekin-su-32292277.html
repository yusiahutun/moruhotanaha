Celebrity Big Brother's Ekin-Su spills on forgotten soap career before ITV fame

 Posted on February 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  569 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Celebrity Big Brother's Ekin-Su Cülcüloglu has opened up on what it was like being a Turkish soap star before she found fame on Love Island.

Cülcüloglu, 29, first came to the British public's attention when she starred in the 2022 edition of the ITV dating titan. Walking out the winner of the show with then-partner Davide Sanclimenti, the pair were together for a total of 18 months before they decided to officially end things late last year.

Ekin-Su has previously let slip some parts of her life as she settled into the Celebrity Big Brother House, revealing to fellow housemate Fern Britton that her previous lover "hurt me" - which in turn was the reason behind the couple's split. Now, Ekin-Su has given more detail on how her career took off as a soap star in Turkey.

Speaking to fellow soap actor Colson Smith, as well as Zeze Millz, Ekin-Su opened up that she had been on a soap in Turkey in 2020. Appearing on the soap, called Kuzey Yıldızı İlk Aşk - or "North Star First Love" - Ekin took on the role of Işıl, who was a regular character on the show.

Filling in her fellow housemates, Ekin-Su opened up on how the shooting had gone for her: "I was literally doing soaps for two years. It was a really smart character, but fancied the main character - but the main character was in a relationship.

"So I had to come close to him as a friend to make him fall in love with me," she added. "But then she got a bit jealous and...yeah."

When asked by Zeze why Ekin-Su did not carry on with the acting skills, the Turkish delight said that her role had to stop due to the pandemic. As a result, she was told by Turkish TV producers that she was going to have to be killed off from the show.

Corrie star Colson, 25, asked Ekin what her death scene was like to film and she beamed as she revealed all for her fellow stars. "She killed me with a gun, she killed me!" she exclaimed.

Ekin-Su then recreated her death on the show with Zeze who shot an imaginary gun at the Love Island winner. Ekin-Su then tumbled down almost immediately, laying on the ground next to Strictly star Nikita Kuzmin who was doing the washing up- in the kitchen, much to the delight of her fellow housemates.

It comes following claims that Ekin-Su is the apple of Kuzmin's eye, according to music mogul Louis Walsh. After all the housemates met for the first time on launch night, the Irish manager admitted he had seen sparks fly between Nikita and Ekin-Su, branding it "a budding romance" to Sharon Osbourne - who immediately shot it down.

Celebrity Big Brother airs every Sunday to Friday at 9pm on ITV1 and ITVX.
