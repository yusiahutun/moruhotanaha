Nick Mullen’s biography

The comedian was born on December 13, 1988, in New York City, USA and is 33 years old as of 2022. When he was young, he moved to Baltimore, MD, with his family.

Mullen’s parents separated after he was born. The star is a high school dropout and therefore did not receive higher education.

Career

Nick Mullen started doing comedy at 16 at Wiseacres Comedy Club in Virginia. However, his popularity grew in May 2016, when he began the podcast with the other comedians, Stavros Halkias and Adam Friedland. Nick is the primary host of the podcast C*m Town.

His podcast is a weekly 1-hour comedy set up around the host’s conversation featuring guests. As of 2022, it is the eighth popular podcast on Patreon. Nick has also gained popularity as A.B. production assistant on local TV shoots.
