Kid Cudi Scraps Tour After Injuring His Foot At Coachella

 Posted on June 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  290 words  |  Martina Birk

Kid Cudi ( DFree / Shutterstock.com)

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Rapper Kid Cudi announced that he’s been forced to cancel his upcoming tour after he injured himself during his performance at the second weekend of Coachella.

“Guys, so, I have a broken calcaneus. I’m headed to surgery now and there’s gonna be a long recovery time. We have to cancel the tour so I can focus on getting better to be out there in top shape to rage with you all,” the performer posted on X, the social media platform formerly known as Twitter. “There’s just no way I can bounce back in time to give 100%. The injury is much more serious than I thought.”

He went on to note that fans will be eligible for a refund for any tickets they purchased for the tour and said he will announce new tour plans as soon as possible.

“We will be back with new tour dates as soon as possible, and I can’t wait to get back with you guys and turn up how we do. I’m so sorry fam and I love you all so much, thanks for the endless love and support. I’m really disappointed as I’m sure you guys are too, but I will be back,” he added.

The now-canceled tour was slated to get underway at the Moody Center in Austin on June 28 with additional North American shows extending through August 30 when the tour segment was scheduled to conclude at Crytpo.com Arena in Los Angeles.

Kid Cudi was forced to cut his Sunday performance during the second weekend of Coachella early after he injured his foot while jumping off stage. He later shared that he had broken his foot during the stunt.
