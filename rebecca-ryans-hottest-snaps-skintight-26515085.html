Rebecca Ryan's hottest snaps - skintight shorts, poolside glamour and plunging necklines

Coronation Street's Rebecca Ryan already had an army of fans when she arrived on the cobbles, thanks to shows like Shameless and Waterloo Road.

The actress made a splash as teen Debbie Gallagher in Shameless and was a hit as Vicki MacDonald in Waterloo Road, which she was in from 2009 to 2011.

But in recent months she has become best known as Weatherfield's Lydia Chambers.

Rebecca, 30, has been a favourite with viewers, and she has also become quite the social media sensation.

The star has more than 58,000 followers on Instagram, largely because of her glam snaps.

We have a look at some of her hottest pictures.

Poolside chic

Rebecca set pulses racing when she shared a sizzling snap showing off her curves by the pool.

The image showed the brunette stretched out as she perched beside an infinity pool during a sun-soaked holiday.

She was clad in a black swimsuit that hugged her curves and featured a cut out that gave fans a peek at her trim waist, while a delicious looking cocktail in her hand completed the glam look.

The shot earned her plenty of love on Instagram.

"Oh my God, you're absolutely gorgeous!" said one fan.

"Phwoarrrrrr you look incredible," gushed another.

Pre-lockdown nights out

The star spiced things up during lockdown by sharing snaps showing her dressed up for a night out in pre-pandemic times.

She cheered fans up no end with the shots of her wearing strappy heels and a short black dress sprinkled with bright flower patterns.

"Dreaming of the days when I can get dressed up again!!" she wrote.

"In reality I am sat in my PJs watching repeats of Home and Away."

Her admirers said Rebecca looked as if she'd been "sent by God" and branded her smoking hot look totally "stunning".

Lycra-clad for park jaunt

Fans' jaws were dropping when Rebecca unveiled a seriously sexy snap of herself in skin-tight pink Lycra.

The clinging trousers and crop top hugged the star's curves and gave her followers a glimpse of her toned tummy as she hung out at the park.

The actress's hair was blonde at the time and she wore it cascading loose over her shoulders in gorgeous waves.

Fans said the hair colour "really suited" the star.

"That's my favourite photo of you, truly gorgeous," said one person, while another said the screen star was "Bellissima".

Cleaning during coronavirus

The star took cleaning to another level when she shared a photo of herself sprucing up her home while dressed in a risqué black top and the teeniest denim shorts.

Rebecca looked incredible as she got to work with a steam cleaner, with her tiny denim shorts and partly sheer top highlighting her killer figure.

Black heels made her legs go on forever, and the actress's sultry make-up job and glossy hair made her look far too glam for the kitchen.

"Sick of getting dressed up for the cleaning, Miss Rona is cancelled," she joked.

Angel wings

Fans thought they had died and gone to heaven when Rebecca uploaded a picture of herself wearing a skimpy white dress as she posed in front of a pair of glowing angel wings.

She looked gorgeous in the short, wrap dress, which emphasised her long legs and cinched in at the waist to display her dynamite shape.

"Always an Angel," wrote the actress.

And her fans certainly agreed.

"Wow, you can be my angel anytime," one exclaimed.

"Beautiful as always," posted another appreciative admirer.

Pretty in plunging neckline

Rebecca's fans got a bit hot under the collar when she donned a rather revealing dress.

The sweet shot showed the star sitting down and snapping a selfie in a pretty summer dress with a seriously plunging neckline.

Many fans called her a "goddess" and a "stunner" after she shared the sizzling shot, and the comments section was flooded with hearts in eyes and flaming hot emojis as her followers showed their appreciation.

"You look amazing, love the look," praised one.

Another told Rebecca she was "so gorgeous".
