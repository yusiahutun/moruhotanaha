Who is Lily Allen's husband? Everything you need to know about Stranger Things star David Harbour

 Posted on July 12, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1012 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The couple secretly wed in Las Vegas in September 2020

    Lily Allen and Stranger Things star David Harbour are one Hollywood's most adorable couples, even though they tend to keep things pretty low key. But when they do share brief glimpses into their love life, like when Lily shared the first look at her stunning wedding dress after secretly tying the knot with David in September 2020, it's clear just how besotted they are with each other.

    After months of speculation about whether the couple were engaged after seeing the singer in an incredible diamond ring on a Capri holiday, fans were overjoyed when it turned out Lily and David exchanged vows in Las Vegas.

    But what do we know about her romance with the American actor? Keep reading to find out...

    When did Lily and David first meet?

    The pop star and David surprised fans when they were spotted together at a West End production of in August 2019. A string of dates ensued, including a boxing match and several PDA-filled outings.

    The pair were seen publicly kissing at Madison Square Garden in October, proving things were getting serious.

    David and Lily only made their official red carpet debut in January of this year, attending the SAG Awards 2020 arm-in-arm. They sure make an attractive couple!

    Do they live together?

    The pair have spent much of their relationship on different continents, dividing their time between the UK and David's New York City apartment. 

    During the coronavirus pandemic, David moved into Lily's London home with her children Ethel, eight, and Marnie, seven, who she shares with ex-husband Sam Cooper. David had been in Atlanta filming , but managed to fly out to the UK to see Lily just before travel restrictions kicked in, admitting it would have been a "really bad situation" for his mental health "to be quarantined alone".

    In 2023, David Harbour gave an updated tour of his home for , featuring Lily as it turned out the couple had moved into a stunning Brooklyn townhouse.

    David described his wife as "someone who lives with color in a deeper way than most. Her taste is bold, silly, fun, eccentric—it’s exciting."

    Lily told Grazia that she loves the anonymity she experiences when she's in Brooklyn, explaining: "No one knows who I am. Growing up with the attention I had in my twenties can lead to, sort of, narcissistic feelings. It’s nice to be in a relationship where somebody else gets all of that attention. It’s good for my ego."

    When did the singer start wearing an engagement ring?

    Rumours first began circulating that David had popped the question after Lily was spotted wearing a ring during a stroll around Chelsea, London in late November.

    Her fans have also quizzed her about it on Instagram, but the British singer has always refused to comment.

    Responding to a direct fan question, she joked: 'First rule of engagement club........,' referencing Brad Pitt's famous line from Fight Club ("you do not talk about Fight Club").

    This could be Lily's second marriage; she separated from husband Sam Cooper in 2015 after about four years of marriage, before finalising their divorce in 2018. 

    What is David best known for?

    David is perhaps best known for his role as Jim Hopper in Netflix's smash hit show . The no-nonsense police chief makes it his mission to find missing local boy Will Byers – only to get sucked into the dark and mysterious world of the Upside Down.

    Aside from fighting Demogorgons, David is also famed for playing the titular anti-hero in. The talented actor has also starred as Gregg Beam in , Shep Campbell in and Dexter Tolliver in .

    Who are the star's exes?

    You might recognise some of David's famous exes! The star was previously in a relationship with actress Alison Sudol.

    David and Alison, who also performs under the name , called it quits a few months before he was linked to Lily.

    The actor was previously rumoured to be engaged to star Maria Thayer. However, the pair quietly ended things in 2011 before they were allegedly set to walk down the aisle.

    In other surprising news, he also dated Julia Stiles for four years between 2011 and 2015 – and they even lived together in New York! Who knew!

    When did they marry?

    Lily and David exchanged vows at the Graceland Wedding Chapel, with the ceremony carried out by Brendan Paul, an Elvis impersonator who owns the chapel. 

    The couple are in good company, as Jon Bon Jovi and his wife Dorothea held their wedding there, while Billy Ray Cyrus and actress Andie McDowell are among those who have renewed their vows at the chapel.

    What has David said about their marriage?

    During the SAG/AFTRA strike, David struggled to keep busy - and he got frank about the impact it had on his relationship.

    "Ultimately, it's very good because you get to know someone", he told PEOPLE about their marriage. "But, let me tell you, my wife likes when I go to work because it's nice for her to have her life and me to have my life and for us not to just annoy each other by being around all the time."

    Despite this, the couple seem to only get stronger. "We have these two kids, and it's so great,” David said, referring to his stepchildren, Ethel, 11 and Marnie, 10. “We get to raise them and stuff, and as our marriage continues, we grow closer and closer.”
