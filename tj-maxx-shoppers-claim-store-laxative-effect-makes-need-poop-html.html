And while it may seem like an odd phenomenon, there is a theory behind the poop madness. 

It's known as the Mariko Aoki phenomenon, and while it claims that people have the urge to poop when entering a book store, it can apply to other locations as well. 

According to the hypothesis, the smell of books in a bookstore may trigger a bowel movement. 

And it's not just book stores, but anywhere that relaxes your senses, meaning that if the smell of TJ Maxx makes you feel comfortable, you are more likely to poop. 

According to Eating Well, when you are relaxed, sensory cells work to analyze your surroundings, and then send a message to your brain that tells it to go to the bathroom. 

Your brain will decide whether or not to team up with your gut and begin to get your bowels moving. 

This happens when you are comfortable, which is why many people only poop when at home or in relaxing stores.
