Terry Ellis, who sings with a three-octave vocal length, has grabbed a lot of attention from her singing ability and her top RnB songs. Despite being in the spotlight for being one of the founding members of the famous singing group En Vogue, Terry has maintained low-keyness in her life.

If you remain curious to know Terry on a personal level and find out what's her relationship status as of now, join us in the following context!

Terry Ellis Married?

American singer Terry Ellis, who currently ages 54, has her fans wondering about her relationship status. While her followers are curious to know about her love life, Terry has made sure to keep her personal life at bay.

No information about Terry having an affair or dating anyone has surfaced in the media. Moreover, Terry is tight-lipped when talking about her romantic relationships till date. Her social media accounts also don't hint on any of her possible affairs, as she never uploads the pictures that could lead to some speculation about her romantic relationship.

As per the shreds of evidence, Terry is presumably just enjoying her singlehood and not into getting married to having a husband-filled life. As for now, the low keyness has further aroused the curiosity for Terry's followers, but it seems like they will have to wait until Terry speaks up on her love life. 

Nevertheless, Terry was previously accused to be dating Denzil Foster, one of the producers who helped to put up their En Vogue group together. One of the members, Maxine Jones, revealed the secret of Terry dating Denzil back in September 2015 after she left the group. 

Though not much of Terry's love life is known out to the world, her professional life remains transparent. 

Terry began her musical career as a member of For You along with her fellow band members Dawn Robinson, Cindy Herron, and Maxine Jones. Later, the band changed its name from For You to En Vogue. The group recorded a parade of RnB hits which even got on the Billboard's Hot 100 charts. 

They recorded the song Hold On that even peaked at number 2 on Billboard's Hot 100 charts. Their album Born To Sing sold millions of copies while becoming platinum. 

In 1992, the group released the second album titled Funky Divas which sold over 3.5 million copies. Terry shared lead vocals on one of the album's top-charting singles "Free Your Mind" which also won two MTV video awards.

Terry Ellis with her En Vogue group members performing during 2017 Atlanta Funkfest (Photo: gettyimages.com)

However, in the mid 90's, the group took a three-year break from recording. During the time, Terry recorded her solo album named Southern Gal and released it on Elektra in 1995.

Back then, two of her singles, "Wherever You Are" and "What Did I Do to You" became a hit and earned Terry an enormous fan following. 

As for now, Terry, who became close friends with Holly Robinson Peete after the two met at the recording of the theme song for "Hangin', shares the good friendship with the people of the entertainment world. 

While reaching the height of success, Terry has garnered quite an impressive fortune for herself, which reflects in her net worth that dwells over $7 million.
