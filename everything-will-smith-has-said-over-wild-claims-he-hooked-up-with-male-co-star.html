A man who claims that he was a former personal assistant to Will Smith says he once caught him having sex with co-star Duane Martin

A man called Brother Bilaal says that he was once a personal assistant to Will Smith. He’s made wild claims in a new interview that he walked in on Fresh Prince actor Will Smith having sex with his co-star Duane Martin. Now, the movie has broken his silence.

Personal assistant claims Will Smith had sex with his co-star

During an interview with Tasha K, he claims he walked into Duane’s dressing room to find the two men in the middle of having sex.

Duane starred alongside Will in a number of episodes of the hit 90s TV sitcom, of which Will was a main cast member. He played Dr Martin in Fresh Prince of Bel Air in Episode I, Oh, Baby, Baby.

The actor made another appearance as one of Will’s poker buddies in season four, in the episode It’s Better to Have Loved and Lost It.

He also appeared in the Boyz II Men music video, I’ll Make Love To You. Duane is known for his roles in Scream 2, Ride or Die and White Men Can’t Jump.

Duane Martin hasn’t responded to the claims over Will Smith yet.

Will Smith breaks silence over the rumors

The Hollywood actor, who has starred in many blockbusters like Disney’s live-action Aladdin and Men in Black, has now spoken out according to TMZ.

A rep for the star vehemently denied the claims. They said: “This story is completely fabricated, and the claim is unequivocally false.”

A source told the outlet that the 55-year-old is looking to take legal action over the claims.

Will Smith has not responded further to the claims over Duane Martin.

Will’s wife, Jada Pinkett Smith, released her memoir, Worthy, which her husband supported. This is despite the fact she admitted that they were estranged and had been for some time. The actress explained this was why she was surprised over the infamous Oscars slap. During the wild moment at the awards show, Will told Chris Rock to not talk about his wife. But at the time, they were already separated, says Jada.

She also called another man (music star Tupac, no less) her soulmate.

“Willow is humiliated by the public way her parents have destroyed their marriage and each other. She’s crying out for attention, causing fear for her well-being,” said a source earlier this year.
