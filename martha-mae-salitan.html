How Martha Salitan rose to Fame?

Martha Salitan along with her husband Erik Salitan appeared in the show Life Below Zero which aired on National Geographic Channel in May 2013. Life Below Zero showcases the struggle of citizens living in the freezing temperatures in Alaska's underdeveloped region. And, the show that has been running for sixteen seasons was what brought Salitan to the limelight.
