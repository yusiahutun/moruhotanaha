Shaquille O'Neal lifts up Judge Judy for hilarious photo opp

 Posted on September 20, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  611 words  |  Larita Shotwell

With a full two feet in height between then, they were always going to be an oddly matched pair. 

But when 7'1 basketball legend Shaquille O'Neal scooped up 5'1 TV star Judge Judy, 75, into his arms while both were attending a Marlins game on Friday night, the image was destined to take social media by storm. 

Shaq, 46, bumped into the feisty TV Judge while at the throwing out the ceremonial first pitch at Marlins Park in Miami on Friday - to the delight of many NBA fans in the audience. 

Scroll down for video 

Lift off! Shaquille O'Neal gave 75-year-old Judge Judy a lift when the pair met at a Miami Marlins baseball game on Friday

Judge Judy, real name Judy Sheindlin, is the cousin of Marlins owner Bruce Sherman, who took over ownership last year, which explains why she was also out on the field.

Photographers snapped the two celebs as they enjoyed a chat together near the stands before Shaq playfully lifted the diminutive Judy. 

Both were all smiles as they posed for the camera, with Judy in an all-white outfit and Shaq sporting a Miami Marlins shirt. 

When the photos started going viral on Twitter, users couldn't get enough of the unlikely new friends.
