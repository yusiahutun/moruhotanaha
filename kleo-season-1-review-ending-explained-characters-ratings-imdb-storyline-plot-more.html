German detective thriller web series, KLEO, had been released and currently streaming on Netflix. The story of the series revolves around the spy character, who has to find out the fraud, who cheated on her and the reason behind all of her suffering. This whole revelation of truth by her will be happening after the Berlin’s fall. Later spy would use her most serious or say dangerous skills to take revenge of all her sufferings from her enemies. The creators of the series are, Richard Kropf, Hanno Hackfort and Bob Konrad. The series includes total 8 episodes in number. Each of the episode has around 45 to 56 minutes of runtime. Talking about the characters, the character or Kleo is played by Jella Haase, and other characters are, Sven by Dimitrij Schaad, Thilo by Julius Feldmeier, Andi Wolf by Vladimir Burlakov, Uwe Mittig by Vincent Redetzki, Anja by Marta Sroka, Min Sun by Yun Huang, Anne Geike by Alessija Lause, Jenny by Thandi Sebe and Otto Straub by Jürgen Heinrich.
