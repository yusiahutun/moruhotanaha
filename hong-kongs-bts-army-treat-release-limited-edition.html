Hong Kongs BTS Army in for a treat with release of limited-edition photobook - YP

 Posted on July 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  263 words  |  Martina Birk

Attention BTS ARMY – an exclusive photobook is now available for fans in Hong Kong.

Fans of the K-pop band who are based in the 852 will finally be able to get their hands on The Fact BTS Photobook Special Edition: We Remember. This limited-edition publication, officially authorised by Hybe (formerly known as Big Hit Entertainment), contains exclusive photos of the group on stage and behind the scenes, as well as special images from the MTV Unplugged Presents: BTS and 2020 The Fact Music Awards shows.

Each book comes with five mini posters and four sets of eight photocards, perfect for filing away in photo albums or decorating your walls.

The theme of this 136-page book is “R for Remember”, and is a message to everyone who has had a difficult year to look back on and take solace in their happy memories. This theme splits into four sub-themes: Remind, Respect, Restart and Record, all of which are important values to BTS. The photos that represent these ideas are special moments from their performances.

Korean photographer Shin Sun Hye, who took the photos, has previously photographed other groups such as Seventeen.

The book is available to pre-order now, until May 9 at 11.59pm. It costs HK$420 (without shipping) and you can order it here.

Shipping will start on May 11, but you can also pick up the book in Kwun Tong on May 13-15, 20-22. Pick-up address and details will be announced on the Sun Entertainment Facebook page.
