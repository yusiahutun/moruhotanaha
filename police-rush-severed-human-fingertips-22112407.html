Police rush severed human fingertips to hospital after cycling accident on bridge

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  322 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Police recovered five severed human fingertips from the Humber Bridge, Hull after a bike crash.

The gruesome find was made by officers from Humberside Police on Friday night after a "cycling accident".

The male victim had been riding his bicycle on the bridge when the accident happened, police said.

He was taken to Hull Royal Infirmary but was said to be in "good spirits", Hull Live reports.

Details of the incident were reported on Humberside Police's East Riding of Yorkshire South Twitter page, which said it had been the second job of the night for the force's Beverley Team Two.

The tweet said: "Second job of the shift isn't something that happens very often.
