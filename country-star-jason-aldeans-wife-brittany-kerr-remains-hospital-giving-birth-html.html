Country star Jason Aldean's wife Brittany Kerr remains in hospital after giving birth

 Posted on July 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  571 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Her daughter's birth went smoothly, but Brittany Kerr's recovery has been far from simple.

The former cheerleader wife of country star Jason Aldean needed a blood transfusion following the arrival of daughter Navy Rome on Monday, February 4.

'When your labor goes amazing… but then things go south, the 30-year-old wrote on Instagram, alongside footage of her wiggling feet. 

Country star Jason Aldean's wife Brittany Kerr remains in hospital after giving birth: 'One blood transfusion and swollen everything'

'Still hospital living with my trooper of a hubs and a precious little girl. One blood transfusion and swollen everything.'

In a second video, the mother-of-two added, to the laughter of her husband: 'We’ve been in the hospital for days now, and I’m ready to take these fat little piggies home.'

The 41-year-old musician and the former American Idol contestant welcomed their  baby girl on Monday, sharing a photo to Instagram of Navy Rome peacefully snoozing. 

New baby: Brittany shared a picture of Navy Rome to her Instagram after her Monday birth

'We welcomed our daughter Navy Rome into the world today at 12:45pm,' wrote Brittany on her Instagram photo.

'7lbs 12oz of pure preciousness. We love you so much baby girl!!'

Brittany also shared a video of son Memphis, who turned one in December, meeting his sister for the first time.

Wearing a big brother T-shirt, the toddler was held in his father's arms as he kissed his baby sister on the cheek.  

In the pink: Country singer Jason Aldean's heavily pregnant wife Brittany threw a baby shower in honor of her then-unborn baby girl and revealed her name, Navy Rome Williams

The couple first revealed they had a second child on the way via an Instagram post in August.

In November, the two revealed the baby's curious name, Navy Rome Williams, at a baby shower in her honor at their home near Nashville, Tennessee.

Jason also has two other children from his previous marriage to Jessica Ann Ussery. They divorced in 2013.

Brittany, 30, and step-daughter Kendyl Aldean, 11, announced the name in a cute video shared on her Instagram story.

Brittany also posted a photo on Instagram of Navy written out in neon lights captioned: 'Today we celebrate you, baby girl.' 

Her name's in lights already: Brittany, 30,  posted this snap of her little girl's first name, surrounded by sweet treats at her baby shower, captioned, 'Today we celebrate you, baby girl'

And right before the official sex reveal of the new baby, Kendyl and her big sister Keeley, 15, rightly guessed that they had a baby sister on the way.

'I think a girl because you've been a bit sicker with this one,' Keeley said.

The couple came up with their little boy's name because, as Jason explained, he's 'always been a big Elvis Presley fan.'

Famous father: Little Navy will be 41-year-old Jason's fourth child. Here he's seen at an event in Las Vegas in September

Brittany was diagnosed with endometriosis, so she and Jason turned to IVF treatment to have Memphis.

'The IVF was probably harder than the actual pregnancy, for me, because it was just such a roller coaster and so difficult,' Brittany told People in July.

The couple announced Brittany's newest pregnancy on Instagram by using Memphis as a model wearing a onesie that said 'Big Brother.'
