Career

Lim began his career in the entertainment industry as a model. Lim obtained a contract with Ford Models in 2009 and relocated to Los Angeles to pursue modeling and acting.

In the second season of ABC’s drama Quantico, Lim played CIA recruit, Sebastian Chen, in a recurring role.

Lim joined the cast of the CBS television series S.W.A.T. on April 13, 2017. Lim was upgraded to a series regular role on September 21, 2017, just in time for the show’s launch. Victor Tan, a S.W.A.T officer, is played by Lim.
