Name to Know: Monday Swimwear

 Posted on September 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  347 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Pictures courtesy of Monday Swimwear

There are few people better placed to design bikinis than this well-travelled duo: living in LA, but originally from Sydney and Hawaii, Natasha and Devin grew up in bikinis – eventually starting their blog, A Bikini A Day. It was this everyday exposure to and the shared knowledge of swimwear brands from all over the world that prompted the business partners and best friends to start Monday Swimwear. You won't find any logos, gimmicks or seasonal trends here; simply ultra-flattering fits, supreme quality and swimwear that you'll want to wear again and again. Here's all you need to know about this unique label…

Who With a combined Instagram following of three million, it would be easy to write Natasha Oakley and Devin Brugman off as influencers with privileged day-jobs, but it is precisely their enviable lifestyles that make them an authority. Best friends, business partners and travel buddies, Natasha and Devin founded Monday Swimwear in 2013, later launching Monday Active in 2016. A labour of love, the collection is designed, tested and promoted by the duo, and its success is thanks to a combination of their unrivalled knowledge of swimwear, unique designs and a somewhat notable social-media presence.

Image courtesy of Monday Swimwear

What A collection of bikinis and one-piece swimsuits designed to provide women of all shapes and sizes with the perfect bikini to suit their needs, with regularly updated colours and styles. Comfort and fit are a core focus, with each piece designed to flatter and enhance the female form. From the Clovelly top – an underwired style designed to provide support – to St Barths, a new style for S/S 16, there's something to suit every shape and size.

Why Comfort? Check. Flattering silhouettes? Check. Lustworthy? Check again. From Brazilian-style bottoms to briefs with fuller coverage, Monday Swimwear has you covered. Striking a balance between classic and contemporary, the label caters for the woman who wants to look great and feel confident on the beach. What's more, Bella Hadid and Kendall Jenner are fans. If it's good enough for them…


Where The collection is exclusive to Monday Swimwear's e-commerce site: mondayswimwear.com.
