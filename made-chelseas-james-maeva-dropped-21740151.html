Made in Chelsea Miles slams James and Maeva as reason behind sudden exit confirmed

 Posted on March 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  389 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Made in Chelsea couple James Taylor and Maeva D'Ascanio were notably absent from the line-up of the show this year, and their absence has finally been explained.

In tonight's first episode of the 19th series, new guy Harvey tells Miles and Tristan that they are not going to be in London any time soon.

He says: "I got a message from James," as he reads, 'Hey Harvey how are things? Things are great with me.

"Maeva and I have been away in France. We're having such a good time together. I'm in love. So we've decided to stay out here for a few months."

As Miles shares his shock, Harvey added: "Yeah they've decided to stay out there and do a ski season."
