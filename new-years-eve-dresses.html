The 10 most glamorous party dresses to wear on New Year's eve

 Posted on May 13, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  700 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

2023 has been a memorable one for fashion lovers - from Barbiecore to brown, Y2K to Quiet Luxury - the major and micro trends have been as pleasingly diverse and desirable as one could have hoped for.

As we countdown to 2024, we have plenty to look forward to, and naturally here at Hello! Fashion, we want to do so in style. Whether it’s a house party or a night on the town, we'll make sure you are covered with the ultimate evening wear to ring in the new year. Feathers, sequins and metallics are all firmly back on the agenda. So pop open the champagne and start celebrating.

How we chose:

Style: From maxi to mini, I've picked a variation of lengths depending on your desired hem. I've also tried to choose pieces that are party season-approved but also boast a variety of detailing and designs. I chose the following and used my fashion expertise and industry knowledge to pick out pieces that are timeless or on-trend for this party season.

Occasion: Depending on your NYE plans, I've found things to suit all occasions, from hosting at your house to black tie events.

Budget:  I've found pieces starting from £65.00 for those who want a bargain, all the way up to around £500, for those who'd like to invest in a statement piece.

The 10 best party dresses to wear on New Year's Eve:

  • Pendant embellished stretch-mesh mini dress

    Clio Peppiatt

    What draws me to this dress the most is the bow-shaped necklace traced at the neckline. It’s such a unique feature that accentuates the décolletage without the need for additional accessories. With its thousands of crystals, it catches the light with every movement, ensuring that all eyes are on you at any event.

  • Gathered Crystal-Embellished Mesh Midi Dress

    Self-Portrait

    No brand does sophisticated evening glamour like Self-Portrait. I love the slinky silhouette and contrasting statement florals, the perfect balance of subtle and showy.

  • Annie Dress Red

    Silvia Astore

    Red is the colour of the moment, and we predict plenty of crimson hues on NYE agendas this year. This stunning dress from Silvia Astore's FW23 collection is fun, flirty and utterly showstopping. 

  • Nicolette One-Shoulder Feather-Trimmed Sequined Stretch-Crepe Mini Dress

    Rebecca Vallance

    Feathers are a festive favourite, particularly this year. I love this blue colourway on this sequin mini combined with the feather sleeve, it's statement dressing galore. 

  • Belini Dress

    Celia B

    The Celia B dress is the ultimate choice for ringing in the New Year for its perfect blend of comfort, flair, and festive spirit. Its iconic silhouette  is designed to flatter diverse body shapes, and I adore the long sleeves which offer a sleek look while providing coverage. It's this magical combination of ease and elegance that makes the dress an exemplary piece for dancing the night away.

  • Celina Gold Dress

    Nadine Merabi

    If beguiling gilded gold is on the agenda, this Nadine Merabi dress is spot on. I love a mini dress to dance the night away on NYE, and the draping fabric amps up the sophistication.

  • Claudette Gloss Ankle Gown

    Needle & Thread

    It's the royal-approved British eveningwear brand we can never get enough of. I love this dazzling sequin gown from Needle & Thread. It's simple and sophisticated silhouette speaks volumes.

  • Contrast Bustier Maxi Dress

    Atelier Ninety Five

    A new brand on the block that we're predicting huge things for, Atelier Ninety Five is already becoming a firm favourite on the influencer scene. If you're after a head-turning dress to continue wearing season after season, look no further than this corseted dress that oozes quiet luxury cool.

  • Cupid Satin Dress & Gloves

    Miscreants

    Barbiecore led sartorial agendas throughout 2023, and we're not ready to let go yet. Gloves were also a major player in this year's trends, so combine the two with this chic and playful set from Miscreants

  • Bandeau Sequin Dress

    It's hard to believe this sequin dress from River Island is only £65. The layered design and maxi length is so flattering.
