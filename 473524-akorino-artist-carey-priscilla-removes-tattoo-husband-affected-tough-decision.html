Akorino model and make-up artiste Carey Priscilla has removed her husband's tattoo, saying it had affected her emotionally.

Carey removes tattoo

The beauty on their family YouTube channel shared that she was emotional after removing her husband's tattoo.

She noted she had received calls and DMs from young girls who loved up to her, and she was their role model after tattooing her lover's name.

Benito not happy
