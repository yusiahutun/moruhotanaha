Bradley Cooper acts 'protective' over Gigi Hadid as things 'get serious'

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  328 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Gigi Hadid and Bradley Cooper are the hottest new celeb couple on the block (we hope). Although they haven’t explicitly confirmed their relationship, one source says things are getting serious.

Over the past month, the Hollywood star and model have been spotted on several outings together. At the beginning sources claimed they were just “having fun,” but it now looks like things are getting a lot more serious than they expected.

Gigi Hadid and Bradley Cooper are ‘getting serious’

The pair, who were first spotted out together on a dinner date on October 5, are now getting serious very quickly,” a source tells Page Six. “Their relationship is on steroids,” the say.

It’s said the celebrity couple is spending ‘every day’ together, and Cooper is interested in more than Hadid’s supermodel looks.

“He finds her intellectually interesting,” the insider claims.

This weekend, Gigi was spotted on a night out in New York with her girl gang, including Selena Gomez and Taylor Swift.

After the night out, The Hangover star was spotted meeting up with the 27-year-old, as they were pictured leaving a members-only spot at 3 am.

Bradley Cooper is ‘protective’ over Gigi Hadid

It seems like they’re keeping their relationship on the down low for now, but the pair have been spotted several times together. Despite it only been a month so far, Cooper has already shown he’s ‘protective’ over Hadid.

In one of their first sightings last month, the 48-year-old was seen dropping off Hadid after a ‘New York getaway’, ensuring she arrived home safely.

It was also reported that Taylor Swift loaned her Rhode Island home to the two, so they could get some privacy away from the paps.

Earlier this week, they were seen holding hands as they went on a theatre date. Body language experts say “Ultimately, holding hands is just another way to create a feeling of safety and strengthen your most intimate relationships,” as per Woman’s Health.
