Ko Moon-young loves to dress up her looks with statement earrings. She’s fond of Korean designer brands, but also has been spotted wearing Cartier several times. She seems to favour hoop earrings from the brand, including the Clash de Cartier hoops as well as the Écrou de Cartier earrings in pink gold.

She also wears bangles, rings and pendants from the Juste un Clou collection.

Ko Moon-young seems to favour pink gold jewellery; from Chaumet she wore pieces from the Liens collection.

The Chaumet Jeux de Liens Harmony pendant in 18-carat pink gold paved with diamonds, as well as the Liens Seduction ring in 18-carat pink gold, both made an appearance in episode six.

What jewellery brands are K-drama stars wearing on screen?

Bulgari

The heroine’s watch of choice is a classic from Bulgari. The Serpenti Tubogas is a signature of the brand and Ko Moon-young wears the stainless steel and 18-carat rose gold version of this watch throughout the show. The watch gets its sparkle from brilliant cut diamonds set around the bezel.
