12 celebrity home renovation Instagram accounts to inspire you: Ronan Keating, Michelle Keegan, more

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  832 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Meet the stars who have become interiors influencers

    While we're often given a sneak peek inside celebrity homes on Instagram, the likes of Ronan Keating and Rochelle Humes have gone one step further by setting up separate accounts dedicated to their lavish homes – and you're going to want to follow them!

    Giving fans access-all-areas updates on home renovations and styling, these celebs are a must-follow for interiors inspiration. Here are 12 to follow now…

    Ronan Keating

    @thekeatingshouse

    Ronan Keating and his wife Storm documented their move into their forever home on their Instagram account, The Keatings House. As well as sharing photos from the (relatively slow) construction of their property, they have been sharing some of the interiors photos that inspired their décor. We can't wait to see more!

    Mark Wright and Michelle Keegan

    @wrightyhome

    Fans have been with Mark Wright and Michelle Keegan every step of the way as they built their dream home in Essex, thanks to their Wrighty Home Instagram account. We've seen endless progress photos throughout the build, and with a sauna, outdoor swimming pool and stunning country views from every angle, it really is home goals! The couple recently finally finished renovating their dream abode - and it's more stunning than we could ever have imagined.

    AJ Odudu

    @homewithaj

    Former contestant AJ Odudu shares her love for interior design on her Instagram account, @homewithaj, which shares photos and inspiration as she renovates her three bedroom Victorian flat. Featuring colour pop accessories throughout, a super-organised storage cupboard and the summer house of dreams, it's every bit as cool as you would expect.

    Rochelle Humes 

    @homehumes

    Rochelle and Marvin Humes have also launched an Instagram account dedicated entirely to interiors. Rochelle mans the page and has since shared various forms of inspiration, as well as a look inside the family's stunning bathroom.

    Billie Faiers

    @homewiththesheps

    star Billie Faiers created an interiors account after moving into her new home with husband Greg and their two children. Her first post showed off the before and after transformation of their beautiful kitchen. We're particularly jealous of the hotel-worthy swimming pool and the ultra organised pantry.

    Lucy Mecklenburgh

    @lucymeckhome 

    Fitness star Lucy Mecklenburgh’s interiors account came as she prepared for the birth of her first child. Before Roman was born, she revealed a sneak peek at his nursery (above), as well as a look at her kitchen, hallway and dining room. When it comes to design details, Lucy has opted for a muted palette of white and grey, with architectural furniture to offset soft cotton and linen furnishings. The star and her partner Ryan Thomas have got the design bug and have since turned their hand to other renovation projects.

    Lydia Bright 

    @lydiabrightshome 

    Adding to the burgeoning group of stars showcasing their home renovations, Lydia Bright started her own Instagram transformation account in November 2019. She has revealed her living room, her loft room, her bathroom and her daughter Loretta's nursery. In Feburary this year, Lydia and Loretta moved into a larger period property (pictured above) and started the journey all over again! 

    Chloe Lloyd 

    @chloelloydhome 

    Model Chloe Lloyd started her Instagram account after moving in with former Union J star and husband Josh Cuthbert in Surrey. Each room exudes character in the form of graphic cushions and wall art, abstract furniture and metallic finishes. Handily, Chloe also tags where she buys everything from. 

    Jessica Wright

    @jesswrighthome

    star Jess Wright’s home is no exception from the glamour we expect from the show. From glass cabinets to dramatic chandeliers, each room could easily be mistaken for that of a hotel- except Jess’ furniture is near enough all high street. Winning. 

    Alex and Olivia Bowen 

    @thebowen_home 

    Alex and Olivia Bowen stole the nation’s hearts when they fell in love on , and now we’re falling for their houses too. The pair transformed their gorgeous countryside abode from top to bottom, turning it into the perfect family haven. The DIY-savvy pair recently confirmed they were moving into a rental home for six months while they wait for work on their brand new forever home to be complete - and we can't wait to follow their journey once again! 

    Ferne McCann 

    @fernemccannhome 

    Ferne McCann launched her home account in 2019 and has since shared photos she is using for her own inspiration, as well as one completed room: her daughter’s nursery. Judging by her existing posts, Ferne is opting for a modern and minimalist vibe with rustic details (case in point: log-burner).

    Kady McDermott

    @mcdermottsmanor

    Kady McDermott made her TV debut on reality show , and then turned her hand to renovating a bungalow into a 4-bedroom house in Hertfordshire. The former star transformed the property into a modern space with “homely” details. The impressive abode, which she shares with her pet pooch, boasts modern interiors with feminine touches and state of the art entertaining area outside.
