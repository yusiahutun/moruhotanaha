17 Scorpio Outfits for the Zodiac's Spiciest Sign

 Posted on April 19, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1040 words  |  Aldo Pusey

There's a reason Scorpio is commonly mistaken for a fire sign. Born between October 23 and November 21, Scorpios are magnetic and sensual, two traits that carry over from their love lives into their fashion aesthetics. No matter how hot their go-to outfits might be, Scorpios are very much water sign people, constantly in a state of flow and change.

"Due to their transformative nature, Scorpios aren’t afraid to take risks," says astrologer Lisa Stardust. "They believe in style evolution so they like to wear clothes that speak to their current aesthetic rather than anything reminiscent of their youth."

With their personal pasts more or less off-limits, it can be tricky for Scorpios to seek out fashion inspiration, especially when trends loop back to recent decades. However, thanks to their willingness to try new things, there are plenty of ways for Scorpios to update their wardrobes if they know where to look. And despite their reputations for wearing only black, "Scorpios aren't afraid of color," Stardust says. "In fact, they embrace it." This sign's love of bold and bright hues goes beyond color palette into prints and patterns, which Scorpios are happy to mix, match, and clash with equal abandon. The one shade you're not likely to spot a Scorpio in is white, which they hardly ever wear, according to Stardust.

Even without any white in their wardrobe, there's no end to the outfit combinations Scorpios can put together, including the 17 looks below.

A Statement Coat

Scorpios aren't shy when it comes to statement-making outerwear. "They’re not afraid to wear a bright-colored jacket or a bold print," says Stardust.

Layers of Lace

"Scorpios love lacy anything and everything," says Stardust who cites this sign's "seductive style and sentiments" as the driving force behind their love of delicate, but provocative detailing. For a statement Scorpio look, try pairing lace pants with a lace blouse.

Sleek Leather Pants

Leather pants are having quite the fashion moment and that suits Scorpios just fine. For fall, Stardust says that leather pants are one trend this sign is likely to lean into.

All Red Everything

Scorpios are good with color, but there is one shade in particular that aligns with the essence of this sign. "Red is the color of passion, which speaks to Scorpio's desirous nature," explains Stardust. As fashion risk-takers, a Scorpio would feel at ease and at home in a fully monochromatic red look.

Head-to-Toe Black

If the color red encapsulates the extroverted characteristics of Scorpios, black signifies more of this sign's inner life. "Black speaks to their need for protection since they’re energetically sensitive," says Stardust. An all-black 'fit is a chic way for Scorpios to protect their piece and slay an OOTD at the same time.

Structured Separates

While they're mostly game to try new trends, Stardust thinks that generally, Scorpios aren't fond of linen or silk separates that wrinkle easily and require more gentle handling. Instead, they prefer structured separates that deliver maximum drama with minimal upkeep, such as peplum skirts and jackets or blazers with exaggerated shoulders.

Chunky Necklaces

If a Scorpio could only wear a single piece of jewelry, Stardust believes they'd opt for a chunky, statement necklace worn with a low-cut or strapless top to show off the accessory to maximum effect.

An Allover Print

Scorpios are bold by nature and they're likely to feel powerful and confident in a look featuring a print or pattern that's as forward as they are. An allover print on matching separates is definitely a win for Scorpios.

A Bright and Bulky Sweater

In chillier temps, Stardust suggests Scorpios incorporate "big, bulky printed sweaters in bright colors or prints" into their outfit rotation. In terms of styling, this sign will likely favor tucking this style of top into a miniskirt to avoid being swallowed up by an oversized sweater.

Statement Rings

Scorpios love to tap into their witchy side and gravitate towards jewelry that channels good vibes. This could be as simple as a quartz cocktail ring for energy-clearing or a set of gauntlet-style rings that evoke the protective quality of shining armor.

A Cinched Silhouette

Scorpios are known for exuding intense sensuality, but that doesn't mean they're always down to wear sheer layers or show lots of skin. One way for this sign to express its sensual side without shivering is with a basque statement belt worn over outerwear. This cinched style can carve Botticellian curves into even the bulkiest of winter coats.

Mod Goth Layering

Scorpios' signature aesthetic can lean towards the moody and gothic (think Jenna Ortega in Wednesday). That said, Scorpios also love the drama of a print. One way to combine these two aesthetic impulses is to try layering a printed jumpsuit or dress under a sleeveless dress in black.

A Leather Button-Down Shirt

This sign's love of leather — faux or genuine — can veer towards the buttoned-up or the vampy. For Scorpios who prefer a more refined look, we recommend swapping out your go-to blouse or sweater for leather shirt.

Classic Knee-High Boots

Scorpios are among the most misunderstood of signs, especially when it comes to fashion. Their sartorial sensuality can often be misread as a preference for over-the-top dressing, but Stardust points out that Scorpios actually love timeless wardrobe staples that can evolve with their changing tastes. When it comes to footwear, Stardust says that a pair of classic and versatile knee-high boots are a must-have for Scorpios.

A Scarf Top

"Scorpios love transformation and shape-shifting," says Stardust. This inclination translates to a love of convertible accessories and pieces that can be styled differently depending on Scorpio's ever-evolving aesthetic. A printed silk scarf worn as a sleeveless top is sure to delight this sign.

A Sultry Slip Dress

"A classic black slip dress and combat boots are not only sexy but totally on point for Scorpio's goth tendencies in fashion," Stardust has said of this Scorpio-approved sensual outfit combo. To add a touch of ruggedness, try pairing a lacy slip dress with chunky boots for a fashion-forward blend of hard and soft.
