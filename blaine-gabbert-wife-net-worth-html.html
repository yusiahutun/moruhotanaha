Tampa Bay Buccaneers quarterback Blaine Gabbert has paved the way to success in his impressive NFL career with his talent and passion for football.

He's familiar with fans cheering him for his performance and achievements, but he recently scored another huge milestone- his beautiful wife.

Blaine has officially announced that he is off the market and ready to settle down. They shared their vows in a beautiful ceremony last year and received a wave of wishes from friends and fans. 

Blaine Gabbert & His Wife- Wedded Bliss

Marrying their college sweetheart is something that many desire. The desire turned into reality for Blaine as he joyously said "I do" to his long-time girlfriend, Bekah Mills.

Blaine and his wife met way back in 2008 at a bar for the first time and began dating after a few months. Since then, they have been inseparable and stood strong hand in hand against the challenges of time and distance.

Blaine had to travel a lot due to work, and Bekah would either stay in her hometown Kansas or go along with him. They overcame those troubling times and are currently living in Tampa.

Moreover, they shared their lovely proposal story on The Knot.

The couple was at Bekah's sister's house on the 4th of July in 2020 and were going to start their morning workout together. To her surprise, Blaine popped the big question right there and was met with a rejoiced "Yes" for an answer.

Very typical for the athletic couple to get engaged while working out!

Blaine is a well-known sports star, but fun fact- his wife was a sportswoman herself too. She used to play for the women's basketball team of the University of Missouri but had to quit because of her leg injury. 

Shared similar interests must be one of the many reasons that have kept this loving couple together for so many years.

Blaine and his wife shared gorgeous pictures from their engagement photo shoot on their wedding website on The Knot, and the wedding took place a year after.

The athlete shared a glimpse of his big day with his followers on Instagram. On March 27, 2021, the ceremony took place in Paradise Valley, Arizona.

Blaine and his wife took perfect together! They had a beautiful ceremony followed by a fun night full of dancing afterward.

It's definitely wonderful to see that these two love birds are still so madly in love with each other, even after 13 years of being together. 

Furthermore, Gabbert is not afraid to show off his dear wife on his socials. Besides his beloved, he also posts pictures of his travels and snapshots from his games.

A loving partner to lean onto and a wildly successful career; what more might a person need?

What is Blaine Gabbert's Net Worth?

Blaine has bagged fame and fortune from his successful football career. He was born on October 15, 1989, in Missouri.

His journey as a footballer started in his high school days. He played for the Parkway West Longhorns high school football team and played for The University of Missouri in college.

He made his NFL debut in the Jacksonville Jaguars in 2011. Next, he played for the San Francisco 49ers, Arizona Cardinals and Tennessee Titans before joining the Tampa Bay Buccaneers.

He has been playing for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers in the NFL since 2019. A recent $2.5 million one-year deal with the Buccaneers has taken his net worth amount a notch up.

Thanks to his exemplary performance on the field, Blaine's net worth is nowhere less than a million dollars. And looking at the excelling pace of his career, his net worth is only bound to ramp up.
