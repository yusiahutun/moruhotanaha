Drake Bell's biography

The actor was born on 27th June 1986 in Newport Beach, California. He is the son of Robin Dodson (father) and Robert Bell (mother). His father was a professional billiard player, but there are no details about his mother's career.

Unfortunately, Drake Bell's parents divorced when he was only five years old. He was the last born child in a family of five children (four boys and one girl). His siblings are Kellie, Joey, Travis, and Robert.

While growing up, he has an interest in skateboarding and thus regularly practised it. He was also fond of attending punk rock concerts which largely influenced his career. He attended one of the local schools in Orange County for his elementary school studies. Later, he joined Orange County High School of Arts.
