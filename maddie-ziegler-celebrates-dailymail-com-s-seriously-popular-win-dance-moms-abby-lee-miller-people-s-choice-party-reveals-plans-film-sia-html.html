Maddie Ziegler celebrates People's Choice Awards 2016 win at after party

 Posted on March 11, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  643 words  |  Larita Shotwell

She beat Cara Delevingne and Kylie Jenner to DailyMail.com's Seriously Popular win.

And as she celebrated her win at the People's Choice afterparty, Sia's protege Maddie Ziegler revealed plans for Hollywood stardom.

 The young dancer was thrilled with the recognition, saying after her win: ‘ I did not think this would happen, this is incredible! It’s very heavy, but it’s awesome.’

And she said the honour will take pride of place at home: ‘In my room, for sure, for sure.’

But she wants to make sure sure little sister Mackenzie doesn’t get hold of her award.

‘It has my name so she can’t touch it,’ joked the teen,

The 13-year-old also spoke exclusively about Sia's upcoming movie as she celebrated her win at Club Nokia in Los Angeles on Wednesday night.

'I’m home schooled and I do an online program so I do school every morning for three hours. I do miss my old school friends a lot but all of my true friends are my dance friends so it was OK to leave school.'

Maddie shot to stardom on Lifetime's dance reality show back in 2011, but quickly became known for dance work in Sia's Chandelier and Elastic Heart music videos.

Dishing out tips for young girls in her position, she went on to say: 'Never give up on what you’re striving for because some day it will happen. I’ve always wanted to do this when I was older and it’s happening so it really can help if you use hard work and dedication.' 

At just 13, the petite brunette has already achieved a great amount, going on to be recognised in the same 'Seriously Popular' category as a supermodel and a Keeping Up With The Kardashians star.

She said of her award: 'I still can’t believe I was nominated for the award. I just have really amazing fans and everyone’s so supportive of me so that’s really cool.'

High hopes: Sia's protegee spoke exclusively of her plans to make a movie with the musician

Maddie said she would keep her award in her bedroom and felt best pleased that it had her name on it so competitive sister Mackenzie - also a star of the reality show - couldn't steal it.  

Asked about her tumultuous relationship with her sister, Maddie said: 'We don't [get along that well]. I would think she's happy for me. She should be. She was probably at dance, so she didn't see it yet.'

The young dancer was looking adorable on the night in a structured dress that kicked out at the waist in pastel colours.

Looking like a perfect ballerina, she wore her hair in an elegant chignon and paired the cute dress with pink kitten heels.
