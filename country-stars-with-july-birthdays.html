See Which Country Stars Have July Birthdays

 Posted on May 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  216 words  |  Larita Shotwell

July isn't just the month for America's birthday. It's also the month that a whole bunch of talented singers were brought into this world and, boy, are we glad they were. We celebrate July babies in this gallery!

The birthdays kick off on July 1 with the late Keith Whitley, who was born in Ashland, Ky. Even though Whitley died an untimely death, we're still happy to celebrate his talent. July 3 was a popular day for talent to be born since Sarah Buxton, Trent Tomlinson, Aaron Tippin and Johnny Lee all share that birthday. Lady Antebellum's Dave Haywood also has a June birthday — he blows out the candles on June 5. Maddie Marlow celebrates her birthday on July 7, Toby Keith on the following day and Rascal Flatts' Gary LeVox on the 10th.

The Band Perry's frontwoman, Kimberly, turns another year older (and probably prettier) on July 12, with her brother Neil singing "Happy Birthday" to himself on July 23. Country music's best dancer and reigning ACM King Luke Bryan also turns older (and also probably prettier) a few days later on July 17. Chad Brock, Zac Brown, Neal McCoy and Martina McBride close out the month's celebrations.
