Dua Lipa and Callum Turner PDA-filled photos have fans 'falling to their knees'

 Posted on April 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  423 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

It’s official, Dua Lipa and Callum Turner have confirmed their relationship to the world with yet another public kiss, and we are obsessed.

The music star and British actor first sparked romance rumors at the beginning of the year when they were spotted hanging out, and Dua Lipa even attended one of Turner’s Q&A events in Santa Monica. They sealed the deal a couple of weeks ago when they packed on the PDA after a romantic dinner date in Hollywood, and it looks like the cute pics are going to be a regular occurrence. We’re not complaining!

Dua Lipa and Callum Turner share a public kiss while out for coffee

The new celebrity couple packed on the PDA this week as they headed on a coffee trip in Beverly Hills, and they didn’t care who saw them.

Turner, 33, could be seen with his arm around the One Kiss singer, 28, as they gazed lovingly into each other’s eyes. At more than one point, they planted a kiss on each other’s lips, with Turner giving his ‘girlfriend’ the adorable forehead kiss.

The pair were comfy and casual as they both matched in light blue denim jeans. Matching once again, they both wore white, tops, as Dua had her arms tatts on show with a chic cropped vest.

Of course, the photos went viral on X, formerly known as Twitter, to fangirl over the smitten couple as one screamed: “OH I AM NOT OKAY!”

“Just fell to my knees at Home Depot,” joked another.

A third exclaimed: “The hot boyfriend Dua deserves!”

“Never seen a more gorgeous couple,” another said.

It looks like the couple has been favorites since the start. A few weeks ago, when rumors were first popping, they were already hailed ‘couple of the year’ by fans.

The pair ‘demonstrates a strong emotional intimacy’

We’re not exactly sure how long the two have been dating, however, Lipa only split from her former boyfriend, director Romain Gavras in December 2023. So although she and Turner haven’t been dating long at all, they’re already showing signs of ‘strong emotional intimacy’ from their body language.

“While the lip kiss indicates sexual attraction, the forehead kiss tells a more meaningful story about the emotional closeness of the relationship,” relationship therapist Laurel Steinberg tells Cosmopolitan.

Sources have ‘confirmed’ this as an insider recently told Page Six that although it’s new they’re “mad about each other.”

Neither pair has publically spoken about the relationship, but these pictures say more than enough for us!
