Whitney Port reveals she has designed her own wedding dress

 Posted on February 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  395 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

    Whitney Port has revealed details about her upcoming wedding to fiancé Tim Rosenman. The former star of is set to tie the knot with television producer Tim in the next few months, and has even designed her own wedding dress for her big day.

    "When I first pictured myself as a bride, really simple, silk, something strapless or spaghetti straps, kind of like a slip dress," Whitney told of her original vision. "As the planning process went on, I decided, 'You know I think I want to do something a little more elaborate, a little more special.'

    "So, the dress has a lot of detail to it, it's pretty full on."

    Whitney Port has designed her own wedding dress for her upcoming nuptials to Tim Rosenman

    Whitney and her fiancé of two years are planning an intimate ceremony that will be a "modern take on a traditional [wedding]. The whole vibe of it is just going to be one big, fun party!"

    The 30-year-old also told that they will "definitely be writing our own vows. We have some family that's a part of it. It's going to be really personal."

    Whitney recently told that her friend and former co-star Lauren Conrad would be receiving an invite to her nuptials. "Hopefully Lauren will make it," Whitney said. "I'm sending the invites out soon, so we'll see [if she makes it]."

    Whitney led fans to believe she had secretly married on Instagram

    The pretty blonde has been teasing preparations for her big day on social media, and led many fans to believe that she had already secretly tied the knot by referring to herself as "Mrs Rosenman" in an Instagram post at the weekend.

    The image showed Whitney standing at a bar in a white bandeau dress with her hair braided into a pretty updo. "Mrs Rosenman," Whitney captioned the image.

    However she later clarified the comments on Facebook, explaining: "Soon-to-be Mrs Rosenman. Just to clear the air, I did NOT get married this weekend. I was just showered with love at my beautiful bridal shower with some of the most special ladies in my life."
