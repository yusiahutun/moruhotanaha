Carol Alt looks radiant at 62 as she puts on a busty display atThe Miracle Club screening

 Posted on September 30, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  929 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

It has been years since Eighties Vogue cover girl Carol Alt walked a runway. 

But the 62-year-old beauty proved that she's kept her supermodel physique while attending The Miracle Club film screening in New York on Tuesday. 

The star wowed in plunging silver sequin mini dress that showed off her ample bust and toned legs.

Carol wore her brunette tresses in voluminous curls cascading down her shoulders, and accessorized with a grey purse and a pair of clear sandals. 

As for glam she opted for a simple touch of blush on her cheeks and gloss on her lips. 

Stunner! Eighties Vogue cover girl Carol Alt, 62, proved that she's kept her supermodel physique while attending The Miracle Club film screening in New York on Tuesday

Carol, who came to fame on the pages of Vogue magazine in the 1980s and was the first brunette to land a Vogue cover, also showed off her spectacular figure over the weekend, when she hit the Sports Illustrated runway show in Miami, Florida.

She stunned in a white swimsuit put her sculpted arms, a tiny waistline and strong legs on display as she strutted her stuff on the runway.

In addition to her one piece suit, she had on a white wrap while shoe free.

'I love it, it was so much fun!' Carol told DailyMail.com exclusively about being at the SI show. 'It was quite a long runway. Halfway through I just couldn’t be straight faced anymore. I had to smile and laugh and interact with the wonderful crowd that was there. They were very supportive and cheered us on.'

She added, 'Women are beautiful at any age at any size - be happy to let your light radiate.'

The event took place at the W hotel in Miami.

She added that the SI team was incredible to work with. 'Especially MJ Day and the whole Sports Illustrated team. They were so happy to have us there, we were so happy to be there, and it was just a great time all around,' she added.

'I got so many amazing pictures, I’m just thrilled,' added the leggy movie star.

'The last time I was on the runway I believe was for Zang Tori’s 30th anniversary about three or four years ago,' she added.

'I also walked the runway in Portugal while I was there shooting for a magazine. I think that was five years ago. One of the designers asked me if I would walk for them.'

And she said modeling is still a lot of 'fun' for her.

'Frankly these days I pretty much walk because it’s fun not because it’s work,' she admitted.

'I also walk for donations to my animal charity.

'Walking with Vendela was a blast. And having the support of all four girls around me while we were out there, it was just amazing.

'And truthfully the swim search models were terrific and inviting and included us and talked to us and wanted to hear our stories and what was going on in our lives back in the time that we were shooting and how Sports Illustrated was to shoot with “back in the day“.

'I got a lot of nice contacts with several of the girls so that we could collaborate online. They were just open and fun and wonderful young ladies.'

The beauty told DailyMail.com in the past that she stays so youthful looking because she sticks to a diet of raw food.

And the best-selling author of books like A Healthy You who hails from New York also commits to consistent workout routines. 

Carol has talked about her diet on her website.

'It's amazing to me after so many years of lecturing about raw food, and teaching about nutrition, that people still are confused about what to eat. Let's start with the basics: if you give the body what it needs - the building blocks, clean building blocks - it can do almost anything. It can prevent disease, it can relieve disease, it can anti-age; it can also self-sufficiently prevent flu, colds, sinus infections, and headaches. All this I know from first-hand knowledge,' she said.

'People always say that as you get older, physical things get worse: that the body starts to break down. Well for me, 17 years ago I was worse than today. I was dealing with so many petty little health issues: constant sinus infections, constant sore throat, bloat, brittle hair and nails, terrible indigestion (acid reflux). And today I have none of these issues,' added the Versace and Valentino model. 

Aside from her recent appearances Carol has been busy with her acting career, including her latest series is Paper Empire with Denise Richards.
