What is the meaning of how was your night?

When someone asks this question, they are simply asking of you had a calm and restful night. Some people may find the question intrusive, so you should ask the question to people you have a close relationship with.

What should I reply to how was your night or did you sleep well?

You can reply with one or more of the collection of responses above. You should always find an appropriate answer for the person who asked the question.

Is it correct to say, “How was your night?”

Yes, it is correct. However, the question may be intrusive for some people. Be cautious when sending it.
