Just when we thought we’d had the best of the Traylor content at Coachella, Travis Kelce has graced us with even more adorable content, after lifting Taylor Swift up at the music festival.

Living out her Dirty Dancing dreams (ok, maybe not) Taylor Swift had a helping hand from her 6’5 boyfriend Travis Kelce to get a clearer view of the stage, and honestly, we all need a Travis in our lives.

Travis Kelce seen lifting Taylor Swift up at Coachella for a better view

As the celebrity couple attended their first Coachella together, fans at the festival were doing their best to get the most adorable content of the two, and they very much succeeded.

In the video, circulating TikTok, the Kansas City Chiefs star can be seen talking to the music star before picking her up so she could get a better view of the stage as they watched Dom Dolla.

Taylor held onto her drink as she looked around the stage area before Kelce gently placed her back down.

Of course, the video is now going viral on and Swifties are gathering over on X, formerly known as Twitter, to fangirl together.

“SHUT UP RN HE PICKED HER UP SO SHE COULD SEE,” screamed one.

“It’s giving… him picking their kid up on his shoulders during the Magic Kingdom fireworks show,” a second wrote.

A third exclaimed: “KICKING MY FEET AND GIGGLING SO HARD RN!!!”

“Oh to have a man that can pick you up like it’s nothing,” another commented.

A fifth joked: “OH MY GOD I am going to die alone.”

The couple carry on the PDA at Neon Carnival

It wasn’t just Coachella that was a witness to the couple’s PDA. On Saturday night, the two attended the Neon Carnival event, and the kisses kept on coming.

The 34-year-olds weren’t hiding their love for each other, after they’d been spotted dancing to Jack Antonoff’s band Bleachers, earlier in the night.

In the video, Traylor can be seen passionately kissing as house music plays in the background. It comes just after the You Belong with Me singer was spotted wearing a New Heights cap to the festival, showing her support to her boyfriend and his brother, Jason Kelce.

“They were meant for each other. I’ll tell you love it,” commented one fan.

Another penned: “I know they had an excellent night!” We’re sure they did!
