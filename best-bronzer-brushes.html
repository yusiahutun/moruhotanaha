The 5 Best Bronzer Brushes According to Sephora Reviews

 Posted on April 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  275 words  |  Aldo Pusey

We’ve broached the topic of makeup brushes several times before, often noting the fact that options are seemingly endless, making the process of buying them rather confusing. Reading reviews is one way to eliminate some of the guesswork, and the products that compel multitudes of people to gush online about them always grab our attention. Having recently shared some top bronzers for both fair skin and dark complexions, we visited the Sephora site to find the best bronzer brushes according to positive reviews. The five below have all raked up 10,000 loves. Suffice to say, they’re worth considering.

Related Stories

Too Faced Kabuki Brush ($34, sephora.com)

A retractable brush with synthetic bristles, this one’s ideal for bronzers, blushes and powders, and swivels so you choose how much of it you’d like to coat with product.

Marc Jacobs Beauty Bronzer Brush No. 12 ($78, sephora.com)

The extra wide shape of this brush makes it great for applying bronzer to both the face and body. It’s your ticket to sun-kissed collarbones.

Tarte Bronze & Glow Contour Brush ($34, sephora.com)

If contouring is among your go-to beauty looks, check out this double-sided brush, which features a precision end to sculpt the cheekbones.

Becca The One Perfecting Brush ($49, sephora.com)

The firmly-packed natural bristles on this brush work with liquids, creams and powders, so you can use it at every step of your makeup application, bronzing included.

Urban Decay Naked Flushed Double-Ended Brush ($26, sephora.com)

Another double-ended find, this brush applies bronzer, highlighter and blush. An added bonus, it’s vegan and cruelty-free, made from recycled plastic bottles.
