Early Life

Born in Russia, Simon Khorolskiy’s parents immigrated to the United States of America in 1992, when he was merely two years old. He was brought up in the United States and completed his basic schooling. Simon connected with music at a young age, being drawn to the art automatically.

Career

When Simon’s relationship with God strengthened, he felt obliged to dedicate his life to compose music for a divine purpose. Simon often took inspiration from the Holy Bible to compose his music, which gave it a personal touch as well. Simon’s music career saw the light in 2016 when he began uploading his music videos for the world to see. To this date, he has uploaded a total of 91 videos on his YouTube channel.

In 2016, his music catered to the average Russian American community in the United States, however, his music soon spread to various diverse parts of the world, gathering ardent fans who don’t have enough knowledge of the Russian language as well. Simon usually releases music that is available in the Russian language, although he has a total of eight songs that are composed in the English language. Khorolskiy also provides English subtitles and translations on his YouTube channel for his fans who are keen on wanting to hear his music but unfortunately have limited access to it due to language barriers.

Khorolskiy has released a total of two albums, both released in the same year of 2018. Other songs that he has released since have been singles. His album ‘Grace of the Lord’ contains a record of 31 songs. The entire album can be heard in approximately one hour and fifty seven minutes. Simon’s music is most often direct quotes from the holy scripture itself. His purpose is to bring the word of God to more and more people around the world.

Simon plays around with pop, country, and folk music genres in his compositions. He also writes original music based on his interpretations of the classic hymns. Since the start of his music career in 2016, Khorolskiy has recorded 90 songs, each with its own majestic music video. Simon features the Pacific Northwest in his music videos, the place where he resides, as a tribute to the beautiful and natural creations of God.

Simon Khorolskiy is currently not signed up to any music label or a commercial company, meaning his work is solely supported by viewer donations. Monthly supporters get early access to his music tracks as well as the latest updates of whatever Simon is working on personally.

Future Endeavours

Simon released ‘Holy is the Lord’ in early 2021 in the English language with hopes to further expand his music in the English language, which will help him expand his fanbase as well, making the Lord’s word reach every person who has access to the most frequently spoken language in the world.

Personal Life

Simon was born to a Russian couple, who immigrated to the United States when he was a young child. Simon is a married man and has five beautiful little children. He is an ardent believer and follows Christianity. Simon has dedicated his life to spreading the Lord’s words through the art that he is most passionate about, singing.
