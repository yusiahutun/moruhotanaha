Priyanka Chopra Drops the Sweetest Family Photos of Nick Jonas and Malti

 Posted on May 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  323 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas have spent plenty of quality time with family over the summer.

The Citadel star recently shared a series of photos on Instagram that captured sweet moments with the couple’s baby daughter, one-year-old Malti Marie, from the last few months.

Some photos show Malti by herself, posing by herself in a cute white and pink floral onesie and baby-pink headband alongside a doll in an identical outfit. In another snap, she looks out a window while wearing a little denim jacket with a large embroidered M stitched onto the back.

Other pics show Malti with family members. In one, Chopra lifts her little girl up on her knee to look down through a large glass door. Another pic captures Malti playing with toys on the ground with a smiling friend or relative.

Instagram

The rest of the actor’s photo dump includes pictures from a family day out in Central Park with Jonas. Chopra wore a white crop top, denim high-rise shorts, a black quilted Chanel backpack, black flats, and a gray baseball cap. Jonas looked casual in a gray muscle tee, black shorts, a white baseball cap, and white sneakers. Meanwhile, Malti stayed cool in the summer heat wearing a sleeveless gray onesie and matching floppy sun hat.

The adorable photos captured the family of three walking down the sidewalk hand in hand, as well as moments in which Jonas carried his daughter in a basket in the park and Chopra monitored Malti as she climbed rocks.

“August magic 🪄❤️✨,” Chopra captioned the post.
