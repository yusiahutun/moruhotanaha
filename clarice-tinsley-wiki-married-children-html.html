Net Worth And Career Insights

Clarice Tinsley has been serving the field of media and networking for over four decades. And, currently, she serves as the co-anchor for the 5:30 and 10 p.m. newscasts on Fox 4. 

Clarice started working as a journalist in the late seventies, and in December 2018, she celebrated the 40th anniversary with her hometown station, KDFW/Channel 4. Furthermore, she got honored with a half-hour special, “CT 40: A Broadcast Life,” aired at 6:30 p.m. on the 4th of December 2018 on Fox 4.

Before joining KDFW/Channel 4, Clarice worked for a Milwaukee TV station. Besides her work as a journalist, she announced the creation of an education opportunity, the Clarice Tinsley Journalism Scholarship, in April 2017, awarded to a Pete Schenkel State Fair of Texas Scholarship college.

Over her four decades-long career, she also got her hands on few movies such as The Good Guys (2010), Wishbone (1995), and Prison Break (2005).
