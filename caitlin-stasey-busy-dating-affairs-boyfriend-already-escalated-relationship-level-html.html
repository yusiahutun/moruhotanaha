Surrounded by the marriage rumor in the recent date with her actor boyfriend, actress Caitlin Stasey got further surrounded by the controversy of being lesbian. Has Caitlin Stasey tried to remove the air of lesbian gossip by getting married to her long-term partner or is she into the same-sex relationship after her separation?

Married Her Boyfriend or Moved On To A New Partner?

In the year 2014, Caitlin started to date the “Rising Hope” star Lucas Neff only after they came to know about possessing the same political views. The relationship got officially revealed when they started flaunting the power couple pictures through Instagram.

 The relationship between the pair was sailing smoothly and was taking its pace when out of nowhere the gossip of the duo getting married surfaced in the media in January after they shared the “Wedding” dropping several hints of togetherness.

The second hint of them getting married,

However, the wedding got officially confirmed when Caitlin shared the picture of her wedding with her man ignoring all the questions raised by her fan followers regarding her marriage.

Followingly right after the marriage, Caitlin shared her pregnancy news through an Instagram post where she is seen flaunting her bulging belly.

Blessed with a beautiful baby girl whose details got never revealed, Caitlin is still married to her husband who has consistently supported in her feminism and the political affairs.

Their marriage is still strong and is evident on their social media handles where they love sharing their lovey-dovey moments.

 

Well, from dating to marriage and from marriage to motherhood the actress has surely taken her love life to another level, i.e., family. With a loving husband and a child, we are sure Caitlin has a much-awaited success inside her pocket which would allow her to accomplish more name and fame in her upcoming days.

Married But The Rumor Of Lesbian Stays;

With so many pictures and kissing scenes with the same sex on several series, the actress had attracted the buzz of lesbian onto her head.

However, the spark further blazed when she spoke about being lesbian and falling in love with random women despite the fact that she is married to a man. Through a source, she talked about having vivid dreams with the ladies and disclosed about keeping her sexual preference secretive as she had no one to guide her. She said:

“I’ve known I was mostly gay ever since I can remember. I know it troubles many people for me to refer to myself as a lesbian considering I have a male partner. “I think they gather that it trivializes the plight of the LGBTQIA community & although that could be further from the truth at this point in my life I’m trying to steer as far from labeling as possible. Compartmentalizing myself only leads to condemnation & contradiction. I’m happier being fluid and I’m happier being honest.”

She further added,

“I would have vivid dreams about other women. Every night I’d drift off into this utopia of women being available to me & knowing nothing other than my desire for them. No one in my life also expressed these desires, no one in the entertainment I consumed, the books I would read, the company I kept. A lack of monumental events shaped my sexuality, masturbating in secret, telling no one, saying nothing, concealing all sexual queries or thoughts.”

Well, the sudden outburst of her passion after getting marriage did create a tremor. While her husband after the confrontation has neither given any reaction nor consoling statement for her sexual desire.

 If the husband had any issue with her sexual preference, he would have either moved out from the place or file a divorce and left. But as no such reaction got spotted, it seems that Lucas Neff is okay with his wife’s sexual preference being lesbian!
