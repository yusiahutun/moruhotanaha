After 2020 (you all know what happened), I haven’t been able to lose my new comfy girl identity. I still live in sweats, still consider leggings to be actual pants, and spend a lot of time looking up cozy slipper ideas. That last one has led me to tons of cute UGG lookalikes, which will allow me to stay comfy in style while still staying in the green in my bank account.

Of course, if you want to purchase Uggs, go forth and do it! But it’s always good to know you have options that are equally as great.
