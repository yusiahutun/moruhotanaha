Enanitos Verdes and Hombres G Teaming Up For Double Bill

 Posted on March 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  374 words  |  Larita Shotwell

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Two of the most iconic Latin pop/rock groups from the 80’s and 90’s — Enanitos Verdes and Hombres G — announced that they are teaming up for a double-headliner tour of North America in late spring.

The 14-date “Huevos Revueltos” Tour kicks off in Las Vegas on June 1st and runs through June 23rd when they close the tour out with a performance at Radio City Music Hall in New York.

The Argentian Enanitos Verdes, currently formed by Marciano Cantero, Felipe Staiti and Jota Morelli, is known for hits such as “Guitarras Blancas”, “La Muralla Verde”, “Amores Lejanos”, “Luz de Día”, “Mi Primer Día Sin Ti”, “Eterna Soledad”, “Lamento Boliviano” and “Borracho y Loco”, among many more.

Hombres G was one of the top names in Spanish music in the late 1980s and 1990s but went on hiatus between 1992 and 2012 when they returned with a tour and a new album. The group consists of David Summers (vocals and bass), Dani Mezquita (guitar), Rafael Gutiérrez (guitar) and Javi Molina (drums). Their repertoire of hits includes “Marta Tiene un Marcapasos”, “Voy a Pasarmelo Bien”, “Visite Nuestro Bar”, “El ataque de las chicas Cocodrilo”, “Chico tienes que Cuidarte” and “Devuelveme a mi Chica”, among others.

Produced by Live Nation, tickets go on sale to the general public on Feb 9th.
