Paris Berelc is an actress and model born in America. Exotic looks, unbelievable acting talent paired with a great personality have brought this actress who has become a model to the edge of Hollywood star fame. She is known for her role as Skylar Storm in the Disney XD original series Mighty Med.

The actress with the fresh face was also in the main cast of another Disney XD sitcom Lab Rats: Elite Force, a joint spin-off from Mighty Med, and another sitcom from Lab Rats. Paris Berelc also starred in the multi-camera high school comedy series Alexa & Katie in the title role of Alexa.

The talented actress has made the effortless transition from child/ten year old to star and is on the verge of becoming a full-fledged Hollywood screen diva. Paris Berelc also graces the front page of numerous magazines.

Paris Berelc Bio, Age

Paris Berelc was born on 29 December 1998 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, USA, the son of Maria and Joe Berelc. Her parents raised her together with her three younger sisters Bless, an actress, Joelie, the gymnast, and Skye, the youngest of them all.

Paris Berelc started as a gymnast at the age of 5. She reached the highest level in the U.S. Junior Olympic Gymnastics Program, level 10, and also won a number of gymnastics championships in the United States. At the age of 9, she was scouted by the international modeling agency Ford Models, which marked the beginning of her successful modeling career. She appeared in advertising campaigns for brands such as Kohl’s, Boston Store, Sears, and K-mart.

In 2010 she was enrolled at the Acting Studio Chicago by Berelc’s parents to promote her emerging talents. Her professional acting career officially began at the age of 14. In 2013 the talented young woman was booked for her first appearance in the Disney XD sitcom Mighty Med. Her entire family had to move to Los Angeles so that her star could appear in the show.

From 2013 to 2015, Paris Berelc played the lead role of Skylar Storm in the two-season Disney action sitcom Mighty Med, as well as guest-starring in 2 episodes of the reality series Just Kidding (2014) with a hidden camera. In early 2015 she was given the lead role of Molly in the Disney Channel film Invisible Sister, based on the novel of the same name.

When Mighty Med ended in 2015, Paris Berelc retained her lead role as Skylar Storm in the spin-off series Lab Rats: Elite Force (2016). Her starring role as Alexa in the multi-camera Netflix original sitcom Alexa & Katie was announced in April 2017. However, the first season of the series premiered on March 23, 2018. Alexa & Katie were promptly extended for a second season in April 2018.

Paris Berelc took her second movie credit in the drama-thriller movie #SquadGoals (2018), in which she played the role of Brittany Gomez.
