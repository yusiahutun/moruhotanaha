Nicole Scherzinger gives a glimpse into her wholesome family Christmas

 Posted on March 17, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  882 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

They spent the lead up to Christmas in the sun-soaked UAE, enjoying their sixth holiday of the year together.

Yet Nicole Scherzinger and her boyfriend Thom Evans returned to the United States just in time to spend the holiday with her family.

The Pussycat Doll, 43, took to Instagram to share a glimpse into their stay, as she revealed she spent time baking and attending church with her family. 

Festivities: Nicole Scherzinger and her boyfriend Thom Evans returned to the United States just in time to spend the holiday with her family

In one typically loved-up snap, Nicole and her former rugby star beau, 36, sat on a bench inside a cabin as they stared adoringly into each other's eyes.

The singer looked glamorous in a cut-out black top, which she styled with a pair of metallic leggings and sequin ankle boots.

Nicole wore her raven locks in a bouncy blown out style, while she opted for a full face of make-up in a slick of deep red lipstick. 

Family photo: The Pussycat Doll, 43, took to Instagram to share a glimpse into their stay, as she revealed she spent time baking and attending church with her family

She captioned the gallery: 'Jesus, chicken 'n' dumplings, & pie! My favourite time of year. Happy holidays y'all'

Another image featured Nicole's mother Rosemary Elikolani and her stepfather Gary Scherzinger, who adopted her as a child.

Also in attendance was Nicole's younger sister Keala Leemon and her husband Dave, along with their three daughters.

This is the second time Nicole and Thom have spent Christmas with her family in America, as last year they shared snaps in matching pyjamas. 

Religious: Other snaps included in the gallery show Nicole and her family in church, as she shared a snap of her mother, father and niece holding up candles

Yum! She revealed her skills as a baker while sharing a snap of a peacan pie she had made

Other snaps included in the gallery show Nicole and her family in church, as she shared a snap of her mother, father and niece holding up candles.

Nicole also posted a selfie while serving up chicken dumpings and revealed her skills as a baker, while sharing a snap of a peacan pie she had made.

She captioned the gallery: 'Jesus, chicken 'n' dumplings, & pie! My favourite time of year. Happy holidays y'all.' 

The stay in the U.S. comes after Nicole enjoyed her sixth holiday of the year with Thom in the UAE.

While much of the world has been brought to a standstill amid the coronavirus pandemic, the couple have managed to enjoy several luxurious trips.

Dinner time: Nicole also posted a selfie while serving up chicken dumpings

They kicked things off in January with a trip to The Turks and Caicos Islands in the Lucayan Archipelago. 

The pair shared several snaps of themselves taking in the breathtaking views at the beach as they rang in the New Year in style.

In March, Nicole took some time out as she and Thom flew to Hawaii where the star was born.

The singer documented much of the trip on her social media as she hit the beaches and spent quality time catching up with her family.

Nicole Scherzinger's 2021 holidays

1. January - The Turks And Caicos Islands

2. March to May - Hawaii

3. June to July - The Algarve, Portugal, Lake Como, Italy and Mykonos, Greece 

4. September - Venice, Italy and the Algarve, Portugal

5. October - Canouan 

6. December - United Arab Emirates

The star spent two months in Hawaii before her next trip saw her and Thom go on a European excursion during the summer.

In June, they flew to Portugal where they took in the sights around the Algarve before making their way to Lake Como, Italy.

It was here Nicole rang in her 43rd birthday with Thom while she also sang at events for luxury goods company Cartier.

Thom treated her to a boat trip for her birthday where she was presented with a birthday cake during an al fresco lunch. 

Alongside the post, Thom wrote: 'I hope your Birthday was as Beautiful and full of love as you are @nicolescherzinger I love you very much ❤️'. 

In July, they made their social media followers envious with several breathtaking snaps from Mykonos, Greece. 

Nicole was back in Italy again in September as she attended the Venice Film Festival and took in the sights around the city.

She and Thom also flew into Portugal again to see his brother and fellow rugby player Max after he and girlfriend Debora Casimiro welcomed their baby daughter Diana-Sofia in August. 

The Masked Singer judge also kept up with work during the trip as she shares snaps of herself from a recording session. 

The following month was another trip away for the loved-up couple as they jetted to Canouan in the Caribbean sea.
