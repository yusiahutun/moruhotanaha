If you mention the name John F. Kennedy anywhere in the U.S., there’s basically a 99.9 percent chance someone will know exactly who you’re talking about. That said, more than 50 years after his assassination, there’s still an intense amount of interest surrounding not only JFK but also the rest of the Kennedy family.

The Kennedy family tree is a complex one, with many of those belonging to the older generations having lots and lots of kids. So if you get a bit confused, don’t worry—you’re not the only one! And while JFK and Jackie Kennedy are perhaps the most well-known of the Kennedy family, there were Kennedys before them and there will be Kennedys after them.
