FAQs

1. Does Jet2 offer in-flight entertainment?

Yes, Jet2 provides a variety of entertainment options onboard, including movies, TV shows, games, and music to keep passengers entertained throughout the journey.

2. Can I bring my own food and drinks on Jet2 flights?

Yes, you are allowed to bring your own food and non-alcoholic drinks on Jet2 flights. However, please note that there are restrictions on liquids and sharp objects for security reasons.

3. What is Jet2’s baggage policy?

Jet2’s baggage policy allows passengers to bring a certain amount of checked baggage and hand luggage, depending on the fare type and destination. It is recommended to check the specific details on their official website or contact their customer service for the most up-to-date information.

4. Can I select my seat in advance on Jet2 flights?

Yes, Jet2 offers the option to select your seat in advance for a fee. However, there is also a free seat allocation service available, where seats are assigned automatically at check-in.

5. What destinations does Jet2 fly to?

Jet2 flies to a wide range of popular holiday destinations across Europe, including Spain, Greece, Portugal, Italy, Turkey, and many more. They also offer flights to some long-haul destinations, such as New York.

6. What amenities are available in Jet2’s Premium Class?

Jet2’s Premium Class offers a range of amenities, including extra legroom, dedicated check-in, priority boarding, complimentary meals and drinks, and access to airport lounges (where available).

7. Does Jet2 offer any loyalty program for frequent flyers?

Yes, Jet2 has a loyalty program called “MyJet2,” which allows frequent flyers to earn points and enjoy various benefits, including priority boarding, free seat selection, and exclusive discounts.
