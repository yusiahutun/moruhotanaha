She's known for her chic sense of style.

And on Tuesday Stacy Keibler proved she didn't need a formal event or red-carpet to show it off.

The 34-year-old blonde was the epitome of casual chic as she stepped out solo in New York.

Looking good: Stacy Keibler is spotted out in New York on Tuesday in a casually stylish ensemble

Love notes? The 34-year-old was seen typing away on her phone perhaps messaging her new boyfriend tech entrepreneur Jared Pobre

Stacy looked cosy in a simple yet effective outfit of jeans which she paired with a cream chiffon blouse and a fitted leather biker jacket.

The former professional wrestler turned model teamed the outfit with a pair of knee length black leather biker boots which were the perfect choice for stomping around the Big Apple.

A pair of over-sized sunglasses shielded the blonde from prying eyes as she ran errands wearing minimal make-up with a large leather tote casually slung over one arm.

With a dark pashmina around her neck it was clear Stacy was prepared for the chillier autumnal weather on the East coast.

Cat eyes! The former professional wrestler sported a pair of the popular cat eye sunglasses

Natural beauty: The former girlfriend of George Clooney wore minimal make-up for the solo outing

Wearing her hair loose and unstyled, Stacy, who dated George Clooney for two years, looked somewhat serious as she strolled along engrossed in her phone.

Kiebler, who only broke up with Clooney in July, began dating tech entrepreneur Jared Pobre in September, although there was no sign of him on her day out.

Last month Jared, 38, treated the blonde beauty to an extravagant birthday celebration that began in Paris, moved to Amsterdam and finished with an over-the-top James Bond-themed birthday party in Los Angeles.

It may have been that Stacy was missing her new beau hence her somewhat sombre expression and frequent phone checking on the solo outing.
