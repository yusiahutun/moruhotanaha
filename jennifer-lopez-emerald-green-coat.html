See Jennifer Lopez Look Glamorous in an Emerald Green Coat

 Posted on February 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  323 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Jennifer Lopez has been on a stylish outerwear streak lately, and it shows no sign of slowing.

Last week, the actress was seen in New York City looking glamorous in an emerald green wrap coat from Milan fashion house Del Core's pre-fall 2022 collection. She wore it over a floral, floor-grazing maxi dress in green, yellow, and white from the same collection, and a pair of white pumps with gold detailing by Tom Ford. Other accessories included a green crocodile-embossed leather handbag with gold hardware from Aspinal of London, jewelry by Pasquale Bruni, and sunglasses from Bulgari.

Getty Images

Getty Images

The multi-hyphenate star has been spotted in multiple showstopping looks on her Marry Me press tour in New York City. The same day that J.Lo wore the above outfit, she also channeled Old Hollywood in a black coat dress from Dior's spring 2022 couture collection and peekaboo heels from Hardot.

Earlier in the month, Lopez was photographed in various abstract coats. In one instance, she wore a matching blue, orange, and green marbled coat, dress, and bag from Fendi while heading to the TODAY show. During another stop, she wore a snakeskin-print coat and matching heeled boots from Roberto Cavalli's pre-fall 2022 collection.

Rob Zangardi, J.Lo's stylist, talked to Woman's Day about what it takes to dress the icon. "Jennifer is glamorous and feminine with hints of that Bronx street style that has become her signature," he said. "When dressing her for the stage, we think a lot about fabric so that when she's dancing, she can move while maintaining sophistication and extravagance."
