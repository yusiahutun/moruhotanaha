This Valentine’s Day, skip the lingerie—and treat yourself to a set of Valentine’s Day pajamas that will earn you compliments all night long. Many of us spend our days prioritizing others, so it’s vital that we take time to love ourselves and indulge in self-care every once in a while. Valentine’s Day is an excellent opportunity to do so. Whether you’re preparing for a fun night with your significant other or throwing a Galentine’s gathering with your best friends, having the ultimate comfy look is fundamental to looking and feeling your best. And Valentine’sDay PJs are the perfect cozy-cute alternative to classic lingerie.

Since there are tons of Valentine’s Day pajamas to choose from, it shouldn’t be hard to find your perfect match. Take your pick from brands like Victoria’s Secret, Old Navy, and Target—all of which offer comfy Valentine’s Day PJs that are budget-friendly and flattering. With tons of colors, prints, and sizes on offer, you have the perfect excuse to take some cute PJs on a small trip to the shopping cart. Plus, tons of our favorites are already marked-down, so you can score them at a discount.

The good news? Though Valentine’s Day comes just once a year, Valentine’s Day PJs can be worn year-round. It’s a myth that they expire the moment February 14 ends. Hearts never go out of style—neither do the colors red and pink. So instead of forcing your Valentine’s pajamas into retirement most of the year, proudly wear them all year long. After all, expressing yourself through fashion is one of the most uplifting acts you can undertake. And isn’t self-love the truest form of Valentine’s Day cheer?
