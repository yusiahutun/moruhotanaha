Kim Kardashian debuts new ombr hairstyle in time for spring

 Posted on May 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  369 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

    Kim Kardashian has shown off her beauty credentials with a fresh new look just in time for spring. The 35-year-old has switched up her trademark black glossy tresses by going for a lighter brunette shade with a blonde ombré finish.

    View post on Instagram 

    Kim Kardashian debuted a new hairstyle on Wednesday

    The star took to Snapchat to show off her gorgeous new 'do in a series of her trademark selfies and a short videos. One close-up shot sees Kim blowing her fans a kiss, her tresses worn down in loose, tousled waves.

    Kim also revealed she had her long tresses trimmed to keep them healthy – but with her hectic work schedule, she ended up having an unusual hair appointment time. "Midnight haircut," she captioned a snap of herself sitting in the salon chair, before sharing another photo of her completed look.

    It comes as no surprise that Kim chose to debut her new hairstyle on Snapchat. The reality TV queen caused a stir when she joined the social media app earlier this month, and has since been using the platform to keep her fans updated.

    The reality TV queen has gone for a new ombré style

    Earlier that day, she also gave her followers a sneak peek behind-the-scenes at the factory where Kylie Jenner's lip kits are created - and her younger sibling has been hard at work creating new shades for her ever growing beauty empire.

    Donning a hair net and protective goggles, Kim gave fans a glimpse of the production process, as well as posing with some of the highly-coveted beauty products.

    Kim revealed she had a 'midnight haircut'

    The reality TV queen isn't the only celebrity fan of Kylie's lip kits – other celebrities including Chrissy Teigen, Demi Lovato and Christina Aguilera have all been spotted rocking various shades of the liquid lipsticks.

    There's no doubt that Kylie has established herself as a force to be reckoned with in the world of beauty. Not only do her lip kits continue to sell out within minutes, but the 18-year-old has also landed major campaigns such as becoming the brand ambassador for Nip + Fab, and collaborated with Sinful Colors on a range of limited edition nail polishes.
