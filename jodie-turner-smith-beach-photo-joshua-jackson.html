Jodie Turner-Smith Posts Nude Beach Photo Taken by Joshua Jackson

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  344 words  |  Martina Birk
  • Queen & Slim star Jodie Turner-Smith shared a super sexy, nude photo for the New Year, taken by her husband, actor Joshua Jackson.
  • The couple quietly married late last year and are expecting their first child together.

Jodie Turner-Smith is entering 2020 in a carefree and blissful way.

The Queen & Slim actress posted a super sultry vacation photo on her Instagram taken by her new husband, Joshua Jackson, yesterday. The photo features the actress nude with her silhouette shadowed against a Jamaican beach background. According to E! News, the photo was taken at GoldenEye Resort in Jamaica, where the newlyweds rang in the New Year together.

“Happy New Year & Happy New Decade!” Jodie captioned the post. “The last 10 years have been quite the ride!!! I laughed, I cried, and, most importantly, I LEARNED. Keep going. May we all see that out wildest dreams *continue* to come true.”

As if Turner-Smith’s sexy beachside snap wasn’t enough to make us jealous, the hashtags she added at the end of her caption served as a cheeky reminder that she’s still riding on the happy high of being a newly married.

“#ItsNotAThirstTrapIfYourManTakesThePhoto,” wrote Turner-Smith. These are rules to live by, indeed.

The tail end of 2019 was surely full of whirlwind accomplishments for the actress. Not only was her portrayal of Queen in Melina Matsoukas’s Queen & Slim praised by critics, but soon after the film’s release, it was revealed she had quietly married Dawson’s Creek alum Joshua Jackson and was expecting her first child with the actor.

Though the pair were rumored to have been dating since 2018, they didn’t make their official red carpet debut until the premiere of Queen & Slim at the 2019 AFI Fest in Hollywood last year, with the news of their marriage coming shortly after.
