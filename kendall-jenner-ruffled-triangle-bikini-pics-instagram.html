See Kendall Jenner Bring the Heat in a Ruffled Triangle Bikini

 Posted on September 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  312 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Kendall Jenner has us all dreaming of summer with her latest bikini pics.

The model and 818 Tequila founder posted another round of vacation snaps on Instagram today, as she continues to share her photo reel from a recent beach getaway. The new pics show the star posing among the palm trees and lounging on the white sand, while wearing a triangle string bikini top and a cheeky pair of matching bikini bottoms. The knitted two-piece was covered in two-tone green ruffles, and the strings were decorated with amber beads.

The style star accessorized sparingly, only adding small gold hoop earrings and a silver ring with a dark square gem. Her casual glam look included minimal makeup and natural beach waves.

For yesterday's photo dump, the model shared a carousel of pics where she lounges on the sand while wearing a stringy black triangle bikini top and matching cheeky bottoms. She added simple gold hoops and a cherry-red baseball cap from Ralph Lauren, while also carrying a cream ceramic bottle in the shape of an 8, one of the products from her tequila line.

Jenner's recent getaway follows a busy few months for the supermodel. She was among the superstars who attended this year's Met Gala, walking the carpet in a Marc Jacobs bodysuit, completely covered in glittering black sequins, and featuring extra-long cape-like sleeves. She was later spotted heading to an after-party in a jaw-dropping see-through Nensi Dojaka playsuit based off of a look first seen on Chanel's spring/summer 1994 runway. The piece included pearl-embellished spaghetti straps, plus a black thong and an ultra-cropped black tank layered on the outside.
