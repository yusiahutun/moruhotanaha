Early life

Claudia Heffner was born on March 12, 1955. Her parents were William J. Heffner and Barbara Heffner. Not much is known about her early childhood as she is a very private person. However, it is believed that she was born and raised in Baltimore, where she attended a Catholic school.

Claudia Heffner grew up alongside two siblings, Holly McCloskey and Charlene Heffner. Not much is known about Holly, though she seems to have married and started her own family. However, her other sister, Charlene, is a bit of a celebrity after being involved in a high profile probate case in the late 1990s.

Charlene had been adopted – as an adult - by a Hawaii billionaire heiress, Doris Duke, who believed her to be a reincarnation of her dead daughter. After the adoption, she changed her name to Chandi Duke Heffner. However, after miss Duke's death, she was involved in a protracted legal battle where she was seeking control of her estate. Career

Claudia is a former fashion model who walked the runway for several fashion brands.
