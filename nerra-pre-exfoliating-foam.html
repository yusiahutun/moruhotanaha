If you’ve ever wanted to elevate your shower routine to new heights, boy, do we have news for you. We recently stumbled upon NERRĀ, a Mediterranean-inspired skincare brand that seeks to make bathing all the more luxurious of an experience, and honestly, it got us intrigued. In particular, we were hooked on the idea of the brand’s Pre-Exfoliating Foam, a first-of-its-kind product for deeper exfoliation across the body. 

The brand describes the product as part-dead skin lifting soap, part-moisturizing protectant, equating it to like shaving cream to a razor. When first applied, the formula releases dead skin cells so they can easier be wiped away by an exfoliating body glove (use it with NERRĀ’s glove for optimal results—the two products are available in a duo), after which it sinks into the skin to deliver rich fatty acids and emollients that nourish the fresh, new layers of skin.

The creation of this exfoliating soap stems from olive-oil based “green soap” found in Mediterranean bathhouses, which NERRĀ modified into a silky, easy to apply foam formula; it contains both Tunisian olive oil and coconut oil. Essentially, the product is the brand’s hot new take on a “centuries-old exfoliation tradition,” so if you want to transform your apartment bathroom into something of a spa, this is one way to begin. 

It’s recommended to use the product when the skin is warmed up and the pores have been given a moment to soften; in the shower or bath is best. Simply apply it over the arms, chest, back or legs and give it 15 seconds to soak in before rinsing with your glove.

In case you need confirmation that this product actually delivers on results, take a glance at some shopper reviews. One person wrote that their shower “looked craaazzzyyyy,” after using the foam. “The fact that sooo much skin/dirt rolled off…Skin felt like I was fresh out the womb after using this foam and exfoliating. And using it the second time, there was a clear difference in the area I got really good vs the ones I was less vigilant with the first time. Definite staple in my routine!”

Another fan called it “incredible,” writing “I am a big fan of Korean scrubs, Turkish scrubs, you name it, I’ve scrubbed it. I’ve been looking for a good at-home routine & bought a hand scrubber to exfoliate during my nightly baths. This is the PERFECT aid—it’s essentially foamed oil (olive oil, etc) and it is such a luxurious experience and makes exfoliating a breeze. A must!”

Grab a bottle of the Pre-Exfoliating Foam for $26 before summer hits!
