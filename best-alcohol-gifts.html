The 18 Best Alcohol Gifts 2024 for Booze Lovers

 Posted on June 1, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1139 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

All products and services featured are independently chosen by editors. However, StyleCaster may receive a commission on orders placed through its retail links, and the retailer may receive certain auditable data for accounting purposes.

Before hosting my annual holiday party this year, I found myself responding to guests asking if they could bring anything: “I’ll have plenty of food and beverages, but extra alcohol is always encouraged!” And it’s true! Nothing rings in the holiday season quite like a festive soirée with plenty of bites and a steady flow of cocktails. Whether it’s a chic bottle of bubbles or a unique bourbon offering to add to the bar cabinet, the best alcohol gifts are always a good idea. And if the night errs on the indulgent side of the party scale, it’s always good to have a few extra bottles of booze to open in the wee hours of the night (this is a judgment-free zone!).

While it may be tempting to run to the nearest liquor store and pick up a common prosecco (to be fair, that’s always appreciated), a more thoughtful approach is to display your personality and that of your host’s. The Mercer + Prince bottle of Canadian whiskey included below, for instance, is designed with two included cups for you and your drinking buddy to enjoy. Or perhaps go for the Max ID NY glassware for someone who lives alcohol-free and just appreciates unique design. Plus, the Segura Viudas cava, which features an ornate metal base, is sure to stun those who enjoy the finer things in life. Whatever boozy gift you opt for, it’s sure to up the festivity of the season for you and your loved ones.

Below, shop our gift guide to imbibe your way to merriment.

  • 818 Eight Reserve Añejo Tequila

    From personal experience, I can vouch that 818 Eight Reserve Añejo Tequila is downright delicious. Following 818’s Director of Tequila Operations David Yan González’s suggestion, sipping this slow either on ice or neat guarantees a smooth, exquisite finish—please, no tequila shots this season.

  • Max ID NY Ghost Tumbler Set

    My partner gifted me these tumblers from Max ID last Christmas, and I loved them so much I went on to buy several more. They come in a variety of gorgeous shades of handblown glass and are a perfect fit for a large ice cube cocktail.

  • Segura Viudas Reserva Heredad Cava Brut

    It’s a very Segura Viudas holiday in my household this year. After discovering the brand at a NYC event at the top of 2024, I’ve been hooked ever since. This reserve cava has a smokey opening flavor with notes of biscuit, honey, and flower petals. And I mean, look at the bottle; it’s such a beautiful statement piece!

  • Mionetto Prosecco Brut

    You can never go wrong with a bottle of Mionetto prosecco. Celebrating 25 years in the U.S., this prosecco comes in a bright yellow bottle with notes of apple peach and honey, plus a refreshing dry finish. Opt for the massive 3L bottle to ensure the party keeps going well into the night.

  • The Ridel Drink Specific Glassware Collection Nick & Nora Large Glass Set

    If you’re anything like me and find traditional martini glasses cumbersome and easy to spill, Ridel has the solution for you. Named after the martini-loving characters from the film The Thin Man, the Nick & Nora large glass set is perfectly proportioned for your ‘tini, allowing the drinker to sip their cocktail without tilting their head.

  • Blind Barrels Fred Minnick Blind Whiskey Tasting Kit

    Spirits expert Fred Minnick collaborated with Blind Barrels for this whiskey tasting kit that allows you to explore the nuances of different whiskeys while sharpening your tasting skills. This kit is perfect for the whiskey lover in your life.

  • Mercer + Prince by A$AP Rocky Blended Canadian Whiskey

    A friend gifted me this fantastic Canadian whiskey bottle from Mercer + Prince, founded by A$AP Rocky. Not only is the bottle stunning, it includes two glasses on the top and bottom, perfect for opening up and instantly sharing with a loved one.

  • Southbound Tequila

    This newly launched line of tequilas from Ivey Childers was crafted to be perfect in mixed cocktails and on its own. Southbound is available in Blanco, Reposado, and Añejo, the latter two notably aged in Tennessee whiskey barrels.

  • 818 Tequila Margarita Cocktail Kit

    This elaborate margarita cocktail kit from Kendall Jenner’s 818 is perfect for the inexperienced bartender. Including a stainless steel cocktail shaker, two etched coupes, and all the tools needed to craft a perfect margarita, this gift is sure to get the party started.

  • Glendalough Double Barrel Whiskey

    This delicious whiskey is aged in two barrels; first in American oak Bourbon barrels and again in Spanish oak Oloroso Sherry barrels. There’s a slight sweetness and vanilla note from the bourbon barrels and a spicy fruitiness from the Spanish barrels.

  • Prodyne Diamond Head Martini Picks

    I love these glam martini picks, featuring a clear diamond head at the end. They add a touch of glitz to your beverage, perfect for the celebratory holiday season.

  • Gay Water

    Never heard of gay water? I’ve got you. The colloquial term is jokingly used among the gay community to describe a signature LGBTQ+ bar staple: vodka sodas! Available in 12-oz cans in flavors like peach, grapefuit, lime, and watermelon, this easy-to-toe canned drink contains vodka, soda, and natural flavoring—without sugar or gluten. The fun part? They’re only 80 calories each.

  • Three Olives Espresso Vodka

    This one’s for the espresso martini lover in your life. Simply combine this espresso-flavored vodka with an ounce of espresso and a touch of simple syrup, shake with ice, and enjoy!

  • Lobos 1707 Mezcal Artesanal

    This sultry, smokey mezcal is made with all-natural ingredients with no additives. It is incredibly smooth due to the aging process in American white oak barrels and PX sherry wine barrels.  LeBron James is a fan and champion of the brand, so there’s that.

  • Horton Ready-to-Drink Cocktails

    This line of canned cocktails from influencer and content creator Krista Horton is perfect to bring to your next holiday party. The party pack includes three flavors: diet kola, lime, and pineapple.

  • Empress 1908 Elderflower Rose Gin

    This rosy-hued gin is distilled with juniper berries, rose petals, elderflower, orange peel, and black carrot. The fresh, floral botanicals create a delicate spirit perfect for gorgeous cocktails this holiday season.

  • Martini & Rossi Asti

    This Asti is the perfect way to end the party since its slight sweetness pairs well with espresso and desserts.

  • Estelle Colored Glass Mixed Stemware Set of 6

    This set of vibrant Estelle Colored Glass stemware will add a bold pop of color to any bar. This bright set is on my personal Christmas list this year, and the pair I currently have on my shelf is always in use.
