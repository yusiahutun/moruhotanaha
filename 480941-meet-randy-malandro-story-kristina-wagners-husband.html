Early life

The actress was born Kristina Kay Crump on 30 October 1962 (age 60 as of 2022) in Indianapolis, Indiana, United States. She is the daughter of Leonard Hale Crump (father) and Carolyn Mae Schwomeyer (mother). Furthermore, Kristina has two siblings, Joe Crump and Dan Crump.

Kristina Wagner grew up with her siblings in Indianapolis, Indiana. She attended North West Middle School, where she discovered she had a passion for acting and dancing. After high school, she enrolled at Indiana Central University, now the University of Indiana, to major in drama. However, she later transferred to Indiana University-Purdue University at Indianapolis, where she majored in theatre.

Kristina got a lucky break in her early 20s when she appeared in commercials in Indiana while still pursuing her degree. Also, she worked with the University Theatre Group on several commercials in her hometown. However, at 22, Kristina left Indiana and moved to Chicago, where she landed her first acting job.

She did not stay in Chicago for long. Instead, she moved to Los Angeles, California, to work as an actress in the long-running soap opera General Hospital. Kristina took a break from the show and returned to college. She enrolled at California State University, Northridge, where she majored in history.

Professional career

Kristina Wagner started acting when she was in college. She worked in local commercials while pursuing her degree. Then, her brother advised her to send her picture to modelling agencies. The move saw her land auditions for All My Children, Ryan’s Hope, and General Hospital.

Kristina was 22 when she portrayed Felicia Jones in General Hospital in 1984. Although she had a hiatus on the show many times, she finally returned in 2012 and has remained active in the show. Also, she has appeared in other TV shows, including:

  • Hotel (1988) as Susan.
  • Double Dragon (1994) as Linda Lash.
  • A Low Down Dirty Shame (1994) as Lisa.

Between 2015 and 2016, she played Lacey in When Calls The Heart. Apart from acting, Kristina is an advocate for wildlife protection.

After travelling to Africa on several occasions and enjoying wildlife, she became a board member of the African Wildlife Foundation, a top international conservation organisation focused on protecting Africa’s wildlife and wild lands.
