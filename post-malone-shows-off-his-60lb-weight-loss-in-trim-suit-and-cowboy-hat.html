Post Malone shows off his 60lb weight loss in trim suit and cowboy hat

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  325 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Post Malone is showing off his hard-earned weight loss at the 57th Annual Country Music Association Awards.

The music event was held in Nashville and the Sunflower singer was definitely getting into the spirit as he rocked an adorable cowboy hat.

Post Malone shows off impressive weight loss transformation

The 28-year-old looked in good spirits as he showed off his new slender frame on the red carpet.

Looking as dapper as ever, Posty rocked a beige cowboy hat, showing off his new figure in a slim-fitting all-black suit.

Finishing off the look, he added a pop of shine with a silver bolo tie.

Post Malone, born Austin Post, recently opened up about his weight loss transformation, revealing that he was able to lose over 50 lbs just from cutting out soda intake.

The star recently opened up about some lifestyle changes he had made as of late, particularly after the birth of his first daughter.

Speaking to Alex Cooper on the Call Her Daddy Podcast, the star revealed that becoming a father made him rethink some of his habits, especially drinking.

“I guess not being able to be there for my baby, which is a new fear,” he explained. “That’s why I tried to slow down on drinking, to take better care of my body.”

Post Malone and his fiance, whose identity remains secret, welcomed their first child, a baby girl in May 2022.

The star teases a country album

His figure wasn’t the only thing the star was showing off. He was also showing fans he has a few more tricks up his sleeve, teasing a country album after his performance.

In an interview with Access Hollywood, the Go Flex singer was asked if he has a country album on the way, to which he responded: “I think so, yes ma’am.”

The rapper released his last album Austin, in July this year.

We’re just hoping for a Taylor Swift collab.
