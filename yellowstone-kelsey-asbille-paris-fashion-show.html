Yellowstone Star Kelsey Asbille Is Unrecognizable in Low-Cut Gown

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  274 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Yellowstone star Kelsey Asbille has traded her casual Montana attire for a high-fashion look.

The 31-year-old actress, who stars as Monica Dutton on the hit Paramount Network drama, headed to Paris for a Saint Laurent fashion show. Kelsey is no stranger to glamorous looks, as she has proven time and time again, but it's always a stark contrast to her on-screen character, who is often seen in casual jeans and boots.

This time, Kelsey attended the Saint Laurent Women's Winter 2023 show and was dressed in a gorgeous low-cut black wrap gown by designer Anthony Vaccarello.
